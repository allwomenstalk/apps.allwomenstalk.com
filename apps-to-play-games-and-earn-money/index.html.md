# Fun  Apps to Play Games and Earn Money ...

All girls need **apps to play games and earn money.**

I don’t know about you, but I’m an avid mobile gamer. I could spend hours playing puzzle games on my iPhone. And I have wasted a lot of time playing such games. Then one day I wondered if it is possible to earn some money from playing. I searched and found one app that allows me to earn very little money while playing games like Freecell and Solitaire. It is called Big Time.

Big Time is a free download from the app store with extra apps that are connected. You can sign-up with your Facebook and start playing. Before I get into the details, I want to make it clear that you don’t earn a lot, but it is better than earning nothing. As you play different games, you accumulate tickets. Every 10,000 tickets, you can convert to $0.10 cents. Once you reach $10, you can send the money to your PayPal. But because you get money from the app, there are a lot of ads, which can get annoying. However, I’ve been using it for over a month and I still enjoy playing. Lastly, on money, you can enter a lottery by submitting tickets and have the chance to win $1000. I have had no luck with the lottery but you never know.

Big Time is one of the best **apps to play games and earn money.** It has multiple games to play. On the main app, there are 15 mini-games. They are difficult to play, however. Only one works best, “Chrono Combine”. “Total Recall” is another good game but you must have excellent memory so it isn’t very relaxing. Besides the main app, there are 23 separate apps. Each app is its own game like Sudoku. When you download one of these extras, you earn 5,000 tickets. I haven’t played all 23 games, but have tried a good amount. Here is a list of the ones I enjoy.
