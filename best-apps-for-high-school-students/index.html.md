# 3 Best Apps for High School Students ...

Who here struggles with school in general? Not just with grades but the stress and everything? I’m about to show you all the **3 best apps for high school students**.

## 1. MATHWAY

![red, text, product, logo, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/p7/tjgtzpcg5b796fb034709917434981_600x315.jpg)

Mathway is a great app for literally anything that has to do with math. You write in the problem and it will come up with a list of ways you can solve it.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mathway/id467329677)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bagatrix.mathway.android&hl=en_GB)

## 2. KHAN ACADEMY

![green, logo, product, font, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/yt/feilqrit5b796fb04dc39729433486_600x315.jpg)

Khan Academy is like an online helper. The subjects range from regular high school/ college classes to SAT and other major exam preps for free.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/khan-academy/id469863705)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.khanacademy.android)

## 3. QUIZLET

![text, product, font, logo, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/ej/kllx8ckx5b796fb015e03675252080_600x315.jpg)

Quizlet is pretty much glamorized flash cards. You can play games or just do regular flash cards.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/quizlet-flashcards/id546473125)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.quizlet.quizletandroid)
