# 4 Great Apps to Help You Achieve Your Goals ...

Do you want some **apps to help you achieve your goals**?
Between work, the gym, girl time, and everything else, women have it hard enough. But that phone you have in your hand constantly can make everything easier. Here are some **apps to help you achieve your goals**.

## 1. Clue: for That Time of the Month

Periods are had enough to deal with as it is. An app to track your monthly periods sounds a little weird at first but it’s so easy that you’ll want to keep using it. Apps such as Clue are a stellar way to get an idea of how your monthly cycle plays out each time and even predicts the next 3 cycles. You can keep track of menstrual symptoms like cramps, check on your last cycle, and even share the calendar with your BFF’s if you really want to. This is one of my favorite **apps to help you achieve your goals**.

## 2. SplitWise: for Keeping Track of Shared Tabs and Bills

Who paid for the bar last time or brunch the second to last time? Maybe the cable bill wasn’t shared yet? When you’re constantly on the move, it can be hard to keep track of who owes what. Like me, you might not be rolling in dough at this point in life to just treat your girlfriends to lunch. Set up different groups and keep track of what you owe, who owes you, and all the above. It’s easy-peasy with this app to keep track of your girls’ night out every week.

## 3. 1Password: for Recording of All Those Pesky Logins

Protecting your info and online accounts is important but doing that is hard without losing your mind. With 1Password, keep track of your passwords and usernames for everything from credit cards to shopping accounts. Then when you need to sign in, just one password is needed to sign in and find what you need. It will be much simpler than finding that notebook or random place you stashed the password last time.

## 4. Notebook: for Keeping Track of to-do Lists and Random Notes

Keep track of your notes, pictures, and other little things you want to get back to at the end of the day or later in the week. Notebook has a special format for each type of note you want to take including video, audio, pictures, and even a sketch option. It’s great for organizing personal goal reminders, recording audio clips you need, or even tracking your next vacation.
