# Apps That Are Useful in Everyday Life ...

Helpful apps that you can use every day.📱

## 1. FLO

![pink, text, font, magenta, logo,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/ab/w7vqm8no5c13a44b2e8b9917091695_600x315.jpg)

Flo is a period tracker app. It has been so helpful. Flo lets you log symptoms every day and the longer you use it, the more accurate it is. It alerts you before your period and helps you with PMS and your period.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/flo-period-ovulation-tracker/id1038369065)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.iggymedia.periodtracker&hl=en_GB2) MUSI

## 5. FOODUCATE

![produce, fruit, orange, smile, smiley,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/es/osfb7ja25c13a44ceae2a605115404_600x315.jpg)

## 6. GOODREADS

![text, font, brand, number, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/it/sqdiyqg15c13a44cb0983019596774_600x315.jpg)

For the people who love reading, goodreads is such a great app to have. You find different books and look at their reviews. Even though you don’t have access to read the book on goodreads you can still read a sample of the book. Goodreads has different sections for the different types of books and genres you like and is so helpful in finding a book.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/goodreads-book-reviews/id355833469)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.goodreads&hl=en_GB)
