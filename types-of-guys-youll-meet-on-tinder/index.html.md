# 5 Types of Guys Youll Meet  on Tinder ...

There are many **types of guys you'll meet on Tinder.** The Tinder craze may not be as big as it was when it first launched, but it’s still the go-to dating solution for the majority of singles. If you’re new to it, it can be a bit of a minefield, but soon you’ll realize there are some **types of guys you'll meet on Tinder** that you should stay away from so you can find the one that's meant for you.

## 1. The Player

![facial hair, moustache, font, advertising,](https://img.allw.mn/content/an/wd/g97szh8459a7aee898429520537082_500x257.gif)

Tinder has a bit of a reputation – we all know it – therefore it’s almost guaranteed that you’ll come across the player. That's one of the worst **types of guys you'll meet on Tinder.** This is the person that is looking for a one night stand/casual fling/no-strings relationship. He's to be avoided at all costs if you want something serious.

## 2. The Undecided One

![chin, forehead, professional, facial hair, entrepreneur,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/u0/vzdjwfqh59a7af90897cc516276789_500x274.gif)

This person may have the best intentions, but they still can’t seem to make up their mind. They like you, then they’re unsure. They think they want a relationship, then have doubts. The undecided one isn’t necessarily a bad person, but the way they go about things can definitely be a turn off.

## 3. The One with Baggage

![socialite, girl, smile,](https://img.allw.mn/content/py/qp/ffuj3xzl59a7af4d4f615463161911_500x266.gif)

Everyone has some baggage. Fact. If you like a person enough, you deal with it. But if there are crazy exes and countless other things they’re dragging along behind them, they’re probably not even ready for a relationship.

## 4. The Friend

![black, photograph, black and white, monochrome photography, photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/rq/d0/vsfgs2hd59a7c2749531e863972008_480x202.gif)

You go on a date with someone and they’re really lovely and sweet…but there’s zero attraction. Best case scenario is that you actually make a good friend out of it. The best thing you can do is be honest – it’s better than stringing them along.

## 5. The Right One

![photograph, man, male, suit, formal wear,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sw/oj/m4ndvffz59a7b0a9e6a52489281064_500x250.gif)

Believe it or not, finding the right person for you on Tinder isn’t impossible and it’s not something you should rule out. I met my current boyfriend on Tinder and so far, things are going great. The right person can easily be in the mass of swipes, so keep your eyes open!
