# 7 Fashionista Apps Youve Got to Have if You Love Fashion ...

No matter what type of [fashionista](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-become-a-fashionista/) you are, you've got to have your fashionista apps to show just how fabulous you are! I would be so lost if I didn't have my Prada, Chanel or VS apps to keep me going! These apps feature all sorts of secrets about the designers and will keep your fashionista side up to date on exactly what's going on with your favorite designers! So, what fashionista apps are out there? What should you have on your phone? Take a look below to find out!

## 1. Prada

_ itunes.apple.com_

Prada is one of the key fashionista apps that every fashionista should have! It's truly one of the best apps out there if you really want to take a tour of the designer's home! This particular app will uncover a showcase of Prada accessories as well as some amazing artwork by Haines!

## 2. D&G

_ itunes.apple.com_

If you are like me and are obsessed with everything Dolce & Gabbana, you've got to give a try to their very own fashion channel! This awesome app will keep you up to date on everything that is going on with D&G and it can even give you some of the hottest looks of the season!

## 3. Victoria's Secret

_ itunes.apple.com_

If you love VS and are really looking for an excuse to shop there anytime, why not get yourself the app on your phone? This incredible app will let you know exactly what sales are coming up and what VS stores are right near you, no matter where you are! It's the premier app to have if you are a Victoria's Secret angel! It's a super useful app, especially if I'm out of town and don't know where the nearest Victoria's Secret is!

## 4. Chanel

![Chanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/06/4_chanel_320x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chanel-fashion/id409934435)

What fashionista app collection would be complete without a Chanel app? This awesome app will actually give you all of the fashion news out there and will give you a behind the scenes peek into all of the different fashion shows going on! It's a great app to have – and oh-so-useful! It is also super user-friendly, which is pretty amazing. I love it and use it all of the time!

## 5. Style.com

![Style.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/06/5_style-com_320x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/style-com/id289380413)

Now, I would be hopelessly lost if I didn't have my Style.com app. It's actually one of the best fashion apps out there because it really gives you up-to-the-minute updates on any fashion happenings! You can watch style videos, fashion shows and even check out any style right there on your phone!

## 6. Gucci

![Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/06/6_gucci_270x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gucci-style/id334876990)

Have you ever wanted the option to buy Gucci right on your phone? Well, now you can! This awesome app is not only a shopping experience right on your phone, but it's packed with all kinds of fashion news and fashion – everything! Give it a try!

## 7. Beso

_ itunes.apple.com_

Finally, the last fashionista app that we're going to explore is all about shopping! Beso is a shopping experience right on your phone, but it's for all of the designers! If you are dying to get the new Michael Kors, you can – right through this handy app!

I love fashion and I think that it's important to showcase fashion on your phone! If you love fashion too, why not try to download some of these awesome apps? What other fashion apps are good?
