# 7 Free Trivia Games Thatll Expand Your Knowledge of the World ...

Do you think you know everything? Well, now's your chance to prove just how smart you are--and since you're only human, you'll learn some new facts along the way. There's always more to find out about the world, after all. Here are a few free trivia games that'll expand your knowledge:

## 1. Trivia Crack

![Trivia Crack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/qy/k8k40kbp54dd82f4c8852915509112_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/trivia-crack/id651510680)
Price: Free

You won't want to put this game down, so get ready to waste a few hours. All you have to do is spin a wheel, which will land on a certain category: entertainment, art, sports, history, science, or geography. The more questions you answer correctly, the closer you'll get to beating the opponent that the app matches you against. But be careful, because if you answer wrong, it's your partner's turn.

## 2. Song Pop

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_
Price: Free

If you're a music lover, you'll enjoy this app. It's basically one big round of "Name that Tune." It'll play some music for you, give you four choices to pick from, and wait for you to decide the answer. It's simple, so anybody can play. You can even ask your parents or baby sister to help you out.

## 3. Quiz up

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_
Price: Free

Quiz Up has over 600 topics to choose from, so there's bound to be a few that you're an expert in. It allows you to create a profile, challenge your friends, and compete against strangers. There's even a leaderboard, so if you're truly the smartest person around, the game will let everybody know it.

## 4. You Don't Know Jack

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_
Price: Free

This app has more than traditional trivia questions. It also contains a few brain teasers to keep you on your toes. If you're still into Facebook, you can hook the game up to your account in order to challenge your friends. Otherwise, you can just play in private and have the silly announcer all to yourself.

## 5. 4 Pics 1 Word

![4 Pics 1 Word](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/sa/n7fp4ff954dd82f52b016667023105_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/4-pics-1-word/id595558452)
Price: Free

This game is a fun one. It shows you four different pictures that all have a certain theme in common. What you need to do is try to figure out what those pictures are trying to tell you. Then you need to type in the word and hope for the best. It'll keep you occupied all day, so only download it if you have some free time on your hands.

## 6. Guess the Movie

![Guess the Movie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/qk/an835c6t54dd82f528043292269673_322x572.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/guess-the-movie/id375276782)
Price: Free

If you're a movie buff, you should ace this trivia game. It's going to show you cartoon pictures that reference movies and it's your job to guess what they are. The only hint you'll get is what category they're in. Some examples are "Latest Hits" and "Best of 2012."

## 7. Celebrity Guess

![Celebrity Guess](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bu/v5/n3q4j1uz54dd82f5d9b52288736410_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/celebrity-guess-guessing-celebrities/id755398884)
Price: Free

This game will test your knowledge of celebrities. It'll show you pieces of a star's face, and you have to guess who it is as quickly as you can. It'll let you see just how much you know about all of your favorite celebs. You'll be surprised at how difficult some of the questions are.

These trivia games will waste your day, but they'll teach you something along the way, so it'll all be worth it. What's your favorite trivia app on the market?
