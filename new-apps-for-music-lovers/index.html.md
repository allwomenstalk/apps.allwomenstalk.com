# 7 New Apps for Music Lovers ...

There are SO many awesome apps for music lovers. We all know about Shazam, Pandora and Songza but those aren't the only apps worth downloading! There are apps dedicated to helping you discover new artists/songs and keeping up to date on all that's going on in the industry. Check out some cool new apps for music lovers that you should definitely consider downloading!

## 1. Soundhound

![Soundhound](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/fh/wvwped30_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/soundhound/id355554941?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Like Shazam, SoundHound recognizes the music that's playing around you. All you have to do is press the SoundHound button to identify the song, see lyrics, add to a playlist, share it with friends or even buy it. Want to know what's really cool? You can also search for music by singing or humming it into SoundHound! That's AWESOME and SoundHound is the only app that let's you do it. This is just one of many cool apps for music lovers.

## 2. Discovr

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $3.99
Tired of hearing the same songs all the time? Download Discovr to discover, listen to, and buy new music. You can even watch YouTube videos straight from the app! The app brings you an interactive map of the whole world of music, making it insanely easy to your new favorite artists. You can even share your favorite ones to your social media accounts, email, and texts. Save the songs/artists that you love by 'favoriting' them so you can return to it later!

## 3. Tunein Radio

![Tunein Radio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/na/jn/omc7xxy0_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tunein-radio-stream-free-music/id418987775?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
With this app, you can listen to the world's largest collection of music and talk radio. It has over 100,000 stations and more than four million podcasts from all over the world! It's a great app because you can also catch up on the latest current events and sports news - all in one place!

## 4. Digital Concert Hall

![Digital Concert Hall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/dw/a3qyc66x_322x551.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/digital-concert-hall/id554616591?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
This one's for all the classical music lovers! You can watch and listen to the finest conductors and soloists performing live or on-demand. There are over 40 live-streams every year in HD video and hundreds of concert recordings on-demand. You can even watch free exclusive interviews with the conductors and soloists and feature-length documentaries.

## 5. Pause

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
This app has everything you need! It's a well-curated music periodical and it contains the best of everything. This includes music stories, features, interviews, reviews as well as charts, audio and video from some of the leading music authorities like Billboard. It's an awesome way to see everything you need to see all in one convenient place. I can't get enough of this app!

## 6. Hook'd

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
Hook'd is a social music app that makes YOU the star. All you have to do is pick a song, record the video, enhance the vocals (that is, if you NEED autotune) and drop the video to a friend or the world! You can even rap or sing your own creations and share the videos with friends in-app or post it on YouTube!

## 7. Crossfader

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
With this app, it's always a giant dance party. It's curated live by the world's largest community of DJs which is so cool! When you want to take a turn as DJ, the app lets you make your own mashups. Just tilt your phone to remix music and if it's really good, your mashup can be featured on Crossfader.fm for listeners all over the world!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome apps for music lovers. If you can't get enough of music, which apps do YOU love to use?
