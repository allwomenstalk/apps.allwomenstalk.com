# 7 Newer Apps than words with Friends? to Start Playing ...

Stop playing games that are meant for one player! It's time to be social (or as social as you can be from behind a phone). Head to the App Store and search for free games that you can play against your friends. It's a great way to make sure you keep it touch if you're forgetful when it comes to texting. Here are a few free apps that you should download as soon as possible so you can challenge all of your friends:

## 1. Brain Wars

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_
It's time to figure out, once and for all, who's the smartest person in your friend ground. Whenever you challenge someone, you'll be forced to play three rounds of random games against them. Some of them test your memorization, some test your mathematical skills, and others test your ability to follow instructions. Whoever gets the most amount of questions correctly at the end of three rounds is the winner.

## 2. Trivia Crack

![Trivia Crack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/mc/euea86se5540d3fbee6de633433615_322x572.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/trivia-crack/id651510680?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
You might've heard of this game before, because it has become pretty popular. It asks you questions about history, sports, art, entertainment, geography, and science. So if you think you know more about all of those categories than your BFF does, it's time to play this game against them.

## 3. Word Crack

![Word Crack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/l6/lip5uxhb5540d3fada713471061891_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/word-crack-free/id566593443?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This game was created by the same people who made Trivia Crack, so you know it's going to be good. It's basically played like Boggle. You're given a set of letters and you have to make as many different words out of them as you can. Don't worry if you're bad with words, because you're also allowed to use power ups that could help you beat your bestie.

## 4. Evil Apples

![Evil Apples](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/cv/f0x34z9x5540d3fcbafa3330835629_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/evil-apples-filthy-adult-card/id645705454?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Have you ever played Cards Against Humanity or Apples to Apples? Well, that's basically what this game is. It gives you a black card and asks you to fill in the blank with one of your white cards. The person with the funniest answer becomes the winner of that round.

## 5. Dice with Buddies

![Dice with Buddies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/gr/uzwf2rut5540d3fada26c788489684_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dice-buddies-free-classic/id432750508?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Do you love playing Yatzy? Well, now you can play it on your very own phone against your very own friends. Even if you've never played it before, it's pretty simple to learn, so you won't have a disadvantage against your pal who had years of practice playing against her mom.

## 6. Checkers

![Checkers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/wb/ar7lkm5g5540d3fb24134928406517_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/checkers-free/id294664915?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Here's another classic game that you and your friends can enjoy. If your buddy lives too far away for you to challenge them to an actual round of Checkers, then you can just play it on your phones. There's even an app that lets you play Chess if Checkers becomes too easy for you.

## 7. Draw Something

![Draw Something](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/zj/zmyf7f8e5540d3fd22b2d556898755_322x572.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/draw-something-free/id488628250?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This is an oldie that was popular back when Words With Friends was popular, but you can still play it today. All you have to do is draw out whatever word the game gives you. Then the picture is sent to your pal and they have to guess what you created. It's fun if you're an artist, but even more entertaining if you have trouble drawing a stickman.

Technology is pretty impressive. Not only does it allow you to call your friends who live miles away, but it also allows you to play games with them. What other apps do you own that allow you to play games against your friends?
