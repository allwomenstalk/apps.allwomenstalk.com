# 7 Job Hunting Apps to Aid in Your Search ...

Job hunting apps can be very useful when you’re looking for a new position. You can use them to look at all of the job openings in your area. If you plan to move to another city, you can use job hunting apps to search for openings in that location too. Plus most of these apps are free and easy to use, so they are an excellent resource to use when you’re looking for a job.

## 1. Job Search by Indeed.com

![Job Search by Indeed.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/xn/wc154r0o_320x568.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/job-search/id309735670?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.indeed.android.jobsearch&amp;hl=en)

Job Search by Indeed.com is one of the most popular job hunting apps. Every month millions of people use this app to look for new job openings. Perhaps the biggest reason so many people use this app is because it is easy to use. You can search for jobs by job title, desired salary or by the name of the company that you're interested in.

## 2. Monster Job Search

_ [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/dkp7WJ)
play.google.com_

Monster Job Search is a user friendly app. It allows you to quickly search for jobs regardless of your location. Once you find a job that you would like to apply for, you can apply for it right then or you can save the listing and apply later.

## 3. Craigslist

_ itunes.apple.com_

Many people are reluctant about replying to ads that are posted on Craigslist because they worry about being scammed. I must admit that I am too. However, it is still possible to find some great job openings on Craigslist. If you find a job posting that you’re interested in, just make sure that it seems legitimate before you reply to it. Remember the saying, “ If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is”.

## 4. Jobs by CareerBuilder.com

![Jobs by CareerBuilder.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/ru/se2j4a1v_320x568.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/jobs-by-careerbuilder/id524123670?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.careerbuilder.SugarDrone&amp;hl=en)

Some apps require you to create an account before you are able to search for job openings. CareerBuilder.com wants to make your job search quick and easy, so they never require you to create an account to use their app. This app also allows you to set up alerts, so you will know when your application has been reviewed by an employer.

## 5. Job Search-Snagajob

![Job Search-Snagajob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/fv/puo9jb3k_322x551.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/job-search-snagajob/id333188676?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.snagajob.jobseeker&amp;hl=en)

If you’re someone who is looking for a part-time or seasonal job you should check out this free app. Snagajob has some really cool features that you won’t find on other job search apps. It allows you to add a 30 second personalized video to your profile. Also, with this app you won’t have to spend days or even weeks wondering if you've landed the job. You will have access to real-time updates, so you can see if an employer is interested in you.

## 6. Job Search-Simply Hired

_ [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/job-search-simply-hired/id730634951?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
play.google.com_

Job Search by Simply Hired is a great app to use when you’re looking for a full-time or part-time job. With this app you can create a profile so it will be easier to find a job that's right for you. Plus, if you find a job listing that you think that a friend may be interested in, you can quickly email that listing to them.

## 7. Job Compass

_ itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_
Job Compass is a great app to use when you are looking for job openings in a certain area. It’s also a great app to have when you’re looking for jobs on the go. Job Compass uses your phone’s GPS to find job openings and displays the results on a map.

These are just some of the apps that can help you find a great job. Have you ever used job hunting apps to find job openings? Which ones do you recommend?
