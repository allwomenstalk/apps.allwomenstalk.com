# Improve Your Love Life with Relationship Apps ...

There is an incredible number of apps available for better relationships. Do you need to improve communication? Do you not have enough time together? In an LDR and need a cheaper way to stay in touch? Things not so hot in the bedroom? There’s an app for that and so many more besides. Here’s a tiny selection of relationship apps and if what you need isn’t here, I’m sure a search will find it.

## 1. Avocado

_ itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_

Hold your shared memories, photos, messages and such in this app for safe keeping and to keep all your memories together.

## 2. Wickr

![Wickr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/nz/id85hepg55a922509df62992486181_322x572.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wickr-self-destructing-secure/id528962154?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mywickr.wickr2&amp;hl=en)

Wickr allows you to send pictures, texts, videos and PDFs privately to your beloved.

## 3. Icebreak for Couples

![Icebreak for Couples](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/xg/z5avzrdd55a9225061317792986682_320x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/4Vg4AS)

Answer questions on the app and your partner does the same, you then compare answers to learn more about each other.

## 4. Call Sweetheart

_ itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_

Pop the number of your beloved into the app and you can call or text that person with the tap of a button.

## 5. Couple

_ itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_

Share messages between the two of you in privacy instead of sharing inbox space with other people on SMS and email accounts.

## 6. Bliss

![Bliss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hg/xy/sujvdal155a922515e3a2350888197_320x568.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bliss-the-game-for-lovers/id433165427?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.gamesforloving.android.bliss&hl=en)

This is the app version of the sexy dice that tell you what sexy move to make next.

## 7. Kouply

![Kouply](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/kb/xfpjtwui55acc44928a56122018731_600x1065.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/kouply/id499184239?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

You can give your partner points for doing nice things; it is good for romantic couples and not controlling or insecure couples.

## 8. Fix a Fight

_ itunes.apple.com_

Note how angry you are and work through the things you can do to fix the fight and make things all nice again.

## 9. Simply Us

_ itunes.apple.com_

Synchronize your to-do lists on your calendars in an effort to squeeze in more time with your loved one.

## 10. Theatre

![Theatre](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/wj/x2ruid0n55a9229d1ad9e791372330_600x294.jpg)

[synaptop.com](https://www.synaptop.com/synappstore/apps/theatre%E2%84%A2/ID-254482)

An app allowing you to watch your partner watching the same movie you are watching.

## 11. LokLok

_ play.google.com_

Sync up your lock screens to surprise your lover with secret messages.

## 12. Kindu

![Kindu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n3/ze/pqqllpm155a922538b37b183269297_322x552.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/VmXt7O)
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=us.kindu.kinduforcouples&amp;hl=en)

There are some things you want to do in the bedroom but you are not sure if your partner will run for the hills, so find out how amenable he or she is to it with this app.

## 13. Honey do … Me!

_ itunes.apple.com_

The shareable to-do list perfect for those with a forgetful partner. Even “remember to pick up the dry cleaning” can be made to sound romantic when you use this app.

## 14. Relation Tips

_ itunes.apple.com_

Simple, straightforward tips to help you improve relations with your SO (and up to 7 other important people in your life).

## 15. Relationship Tips

_ play.google.com_

## 18. Between

## 19. Sesame Gifts

![Sesame Gifts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/cf/dnifarvj55a92256acd4a477012965_322x572.jpg)
