# Christmas Apps to Make the Most of the Festive Season ...

Excited for Christmas this year? Me too! Here are a few of my favourite Christmas apps - be quick to download them so you can enjoy all the fun they offer too! Some of the apps below are paid but a lot are free to download as well 😃 📱

## 1. GlowTunes Christmas

_ itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_
Is anyone else's favourite part of Christmas getting to decorate the tree and make it look super pretty with gorgeous colourful lights?! With this app, you can extend the fun and decorate your very own virtual Christmas tree! Have fun placing lights all around and finish off with adding some cool snowflake effects to make it really feel like Christmas on your phone!

## 2. Sleeps to Christmas Lite - Christmas Countdown

![santa claus, snowman, cartoon, illustration, fictional character,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qv/dv/gs7jq0745846179d78192747405564_500x375.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/K0YRFx)
Want to know exactly how many sleeps there are until Christmas? This was always my favourite part when I was little. With this app you can get either Santa or a snowman to help you count down the sleeps until your fave day of the year! Don't forget to add a little holiday music to play in the background from the jukebox!

## 3. Twas the Night before Christmas

![art, screenshot, vehicle, Beforg, Origines,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/jt/cp8nt4345846169a6545f132152584_480x303.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/twas-night-before-christmas/id402895364)
This fun app is a digital storybook, allowing you to choose the narration voice or even record your own voice as you read the poem aloud! Don't forget to keep your eyes peeled for all the super cute animations that pop up on screen as you read!

## 4. How the Grinch Stole Christmas

![How the Grinch Stole Christmas! (1966), How the Grinch Stole Christmas! (1966), Grinch, advertising, cartoon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/jl/xvh04ak95846163d007c5467233257_600x375.jpg)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.oceanhouse_media.grinch_app)
Who doesn't love watching HTGSC every Christmas?! Thanks to this clever app, you can read the story on your phone anywhere you go! Enjoy the digital interaction included as you read the story and follow the Grinch on his journey through his least favourite time of year!

## 5. Where's Santa?

_ itunes.apple.com_
Ever wondered where Santa is, right now in this very moment? Or how he manages to get around to everyone in time for Christmas day? Well, with this app you can track his location and exact whereabouts at any time! There's puzzles and anagrams to be solved with every location!

## 6. Letter to Santa Claus

_ itunes.apple.com_
Got something on your mind that you want to tell Santa? Or want to share your Christmas list so that he'll know exactly what to get you this year? Using this app you can do exactly that! This is a great app for kids that lets them write to Santa, and part of the fun is that he'll actually reply!

## 7. Christmas!!

_ itunes.apple.com_
This app is a must-have for Christmas this year! You'll be able to do so many things from flicking through an advent calendar, making your very own gift list, listening to festive and cheery songs, playing fun games and so much more! You'll find everything you adore about Christmas on this app!

Which Christmas apps do you love for this super festive time of year? Comment below and share your thoughts!
