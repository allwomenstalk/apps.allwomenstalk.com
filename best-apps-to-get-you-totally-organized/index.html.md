# 7 Best Apps to Get You Totally Organized ...

Getting organized is never an easy task. It’s hard to tackle that to do list and finish everything before the day is over. If you have kids, it’s even worse. Luckily, with the rise of technology, we have lots of tools at our fingertips for getting and staying organized in all areas of life. Download one of these handy apps and you will reduce your frustration level while also taking control of the chaos, once and for all.

## 1. Awesome Note

![Awesome Note](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/yd/b4feh5tt55564a17c3cd9609803627_320x568.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/lVrx3u)
$3.99

This app is one that you’ll quickly come to rely on for all your needs. It allows you to keep track of your schedule and to-do list in one handy place. You’ll never be running late again, leaving you more time to organize your cabinets and closets. Reviewers rave about how easy it is to use and how much it has streamlined their entire lives.

## 2. EasilyDo

_ itunes.apple.com_
Free

If you’ve ever wanted your own personal assistant, you need this app. It links to a variety of sources and keeps you on top of your calendar and to-dos. But that’s not all. When you have a gap in your schedule, the app will remind or ask you if you want to complete a task that needs to be done. Maybe it’s adding contacts to your list or sending an email to someone with an upcoming birthday. Whatever it is, you’ll never forget anything again.

## 3. Evernote Scannable

![Evernote Scannable](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/ft/f4sq6lfd55564a194e516841540166_322x572.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/evernote-scannable/id883338188)
Free

If you own a business or keep track of a lot of contacts and information at the office, you need this app. It allows you to scan business cards, receipts, invoices, contracts and any other documents and save them to your device. Not only will you have everything you need right at your fingertips, but you’ll reduce the need to carry around all that paperwork everywhere you go. Wonderful!

## 4. Ifttt

![Ifttt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/dp/hp3k2vac55564a191d1b4005969023_322x572.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/if-by-ifttt/id660944635)
Free

It stands for “if that, then this,” and it truly is a life changing app. If you rely on a range of devices to manage your life, you will love how this app streamlines them all into one manageable place. For example, if you have a bed that’s linked to your smartphone, ifttt will also turn off the lights for you. You can hook up all your tech to this app so that your entire life is at your fingertips.

## 5. Timeful

_ itunes.apple.com_
Free

Do you have trouble fitting everything into your day? Don’t we all! This app will save the day. It allows you to schedule time for the things you want to do in addition to the things you have to get done. The app can suggest the best time for certain activities and will help you fit everything into the day that you need and want to. What more could a girl ask for?

## 6. Ringya

_ itunes.apple.com_
Free

If your life is ruled by lists, you will love what this app can do for you. You can snap photos of all the lists you need to keep track of, then organize and share them as needed. No more forgetting to send your child to school without all his supplies or showing up at an important business lunch without all the necessary paperwork.

## 7. LaLa Lunchbox

![LaLa Lunchbox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/xq/e1cpv7go55564a1b443cd770415841_320x568.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/lala-lunchbox-fun-lunch-planning/id519037961)
Free

Whether you only have your own brown bag to think about, or you also pack lunches for your kids and spouse, you will love how easy this app makes it. Packing lunches is one of my least favorite chores, so anything that makes it easier is just what I need. This app lets you and your kids choose what you want for lunch, then creates a handy shopping list for you. That way you always have a lunch and no one ever goes hungry.

Which of these apps are you planning to download right now? What other organization apps should we add to our smartphones?
