Online dating is now more popular than ever before. Nowadays, more than one in three marriages begin online, and more than one in two relationships start online. Currently, Tinder is worth more than $1.6 billion and it has been downloaded over 100 million times. Those numbers are rising every day, and now you’re probably starting to see the real potential in this dating app. Whether you’re looking to forge a new relationship, or simply to hook up, Tinder will almost certainly help you out.

Yes, celebrities are just like you and I, albeit much richer.
