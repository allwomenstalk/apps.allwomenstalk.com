# 7 Things to Know about Using Skincare Apps ...

There are several skincare apps available, but that doesn’t mean they are a given for you. Sure, they can go a long way towards educating you about proper skincare and reminding you to complete your skincare routine. At the same time, they are most definitely not a replacement for seeing your dermatologist on a regular basis. However, skincare apps can be a great addition to your skincare routine and understanding them can make them as beneficial as possible.

## 1. You Can Learn a Lot

There’s no shortage of skincare apps out there, which is great news because that means you can probably find the one that suits your needs best. There are apps that educate you about the ingredients used in your skincare apps and apps that help you choose the right products for your skin. No matter which one you choose, you are sure to learn something from it.

## 2. You’ll Get Recommendations

Some skincare apps will provide you with recommendations for products to try for your skin. This can be helpful if you’re looking for something new or have recently developed a new skin condition that needs specialized products. While the info provided by these apps can be valuable, it’s best to ask your doctor before using anything new on your skin.

## 3. Provides Information on Skincare Companies

If you’re in the market for a specific kind of skincare item, there are apps that give you an entire database of those that apply to your wants and needs. For example, if you want only organic skincare or items that aren’t tested on animals, there are apps that can help you find those things.

## 4. Skin Cancer Information

There are apps out there that provide you with reminders to do your monthly skin check. This is helpful because keeping a close eye on your skin can help you detect any changes or spots that need to be looked at by a dermatologist. Other apps will give you information on the day’s UV rays and some even store photos of your moles so you can compare them from month to month. You still need to see the doctor once a year though.

## 5. Recipes for Homemade Skincare

Many people prefer to use skincare products that contain minimal ingredients and that can be made right at home. This is great because you can control what goes into your skincare routine and can even save some money. You can easily download apps that give you recipes for lotions, creams, serums, body scrubs and much more. By using recipes you don’t have to do as much experimenting on your own.

## 6. Get Helpful Tips

If you’ve been coasting through life with a cleanser and a facial cream, but want to do more for your skin, now is the time to do it because you can find apps with lots of tips and tricks. This is incredibly helpful as you develop a new routine or search for new products to try.

## 7. Anti-Aging Benefits

If you’re anything like me, you’re fighting wrinkles and fine lines. No one wants to look older than they are, right? There’s a certain grace and beauty to aging, but let’s be honest – we still want to slow it down. There are a couple of apps that can help you find products to fight the signs of aging and some will provide you with lifestyle tips and tricks to keep those signs at bay as long as possible.

What skincare apps do you use? They vary depending on whether you’re an Android or Apple user, but a search in your app store can help you find the one you love best. Let me know how it works out for you.
