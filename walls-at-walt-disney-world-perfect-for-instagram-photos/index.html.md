# 13 Walls at Disney World Perfect for Instagram Photos ...

There are lots of **walls at Walt Disney World perfect for Instagram photos**.

Do you have a trip to Disney World planned soon? If so, you won’t have a shortage of backgrounds for your Instagram pictures! All over the property, there are colorful and beautiful walls that Disney fans and Instagram influencers alike take pictures in front of. If you’re going to Disney World, here are 13 of the best **walls at Walt Disney World perfect for Instagram photos**.

## 1. The Purple Wall

![clothing, white, footwear, shoulder, standing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/mn/sk8f6xxq5a128d1ce3db1333640458_600x800.jpg)

The Purple Wall is probably the most recognizable and famous of all the Disney walls. It’s simple and goes with every outfit! No girl’s trip to Disney World is complete without a picture in front of it! So add it at the top of your list of **walls at Walt Disney World perfect for Instagram photos**.
Where to Find it: Tomorrowland in Magic Kingdom.

## 2. The Ice Cream Wall

![dairy product, ice cream, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/fd/pp1bfjd55a128d1de3edd733292326_600x800.jpg)

Fun and summery, this wall goes perfectly with Florida weather and, of course, an ice cream cone!
Where to Find it: On the Boardwalk, right outside Ample Hills Creamery!

## 3. The Moss Wall

![blue, green, pink, yellow, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/kj/kgw9ojlp5a128d1e3aa7b429744049_564x704.jpg)

One of the newest walls that joins the ranks, the Moss Wall is very outdoorsy and natural looking.
Where to Find it: Pandora in Animal Kingdom.

## 4. The Tangled Restroom Wall

![flower, flora, art, leaf, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/oa/h0375d5l5a128d1e6580b600373439_600x800.jpg)

Adorned with artwork from “Tangled,” this wall is a little trickier to find and get a picture in front of, but the outcome is so worth it!

Where to Find it: Inside the Women’s Room in the Tangled Restrooms in Magic Kingdom (between It’s a Small World and the Haunted Mansion).

## 5. The Boardwalk Wall

![advertising, banner, signage, font, sign,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/os/b3/kb9edlap5a128d1e8837a836787595_600x450.jpg)

I absolutely love the old-timey feel of this fun wall!
Where to Find it: The Boardwalk Resort, by the stairs headed to the boardwalk.

## 6. Harambe Market

![photograph, youth, photography, girl, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/li/ry/gyk4lc585a128d1eaffa4854880595_564x847.jpg)

One of the most beloved walls in Disney, the wall found in Harambe Market features an adorable Mickey Mouse!
Where to Find it: Harambe Market in Animal Kingdom.

## 7. The Carousel of Progress Wall

![clothing, red, fashion accessory, headgear, tartan,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/my/df/fmylmtal5a128d1f8995a425517662_564x752.jpg)

Another lovely colorful wall!
Where to Find it: The Carousel of Progress in Tomorrowland in Magic Kingdom.

## 8. The Bubblegum Wall

![t shirt, red, pink, youth, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/ql/b7ojt6zr5a128d20743ec296181667_600x800.jpg)

This bright and colorful wall is just an explosion of excitement and joy!
Where to Find it: EPCOT, Right beneath Spaceship Earth!

## 9. The Morocco Wall

![hair, face, facial expression, human hair color, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/zt/b24rrpbt5a128d2166a78811495386_600x800.jpg)

This beautiful mosaic makes for a beautiful background for any picture!
Where to Find it: Outside of Restaurant Marrakesh in Morroco, inside the World Showcase in Epcot.

## 10. The Children of the World Wall

![bicycle, road bicycle, land vehicle, vehicle, bicycle accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tt/fl/whhvmwcw5a12e16006c86193344080_500x675.jpg)

Featuring an inspiring quote and a whimsical bicycle, this is the perfect location for your next Instagram post.
Where to Find it: Harambe Market in Animal Kingdom.

## 11. The Toothpaste Wall

![blue, cobalt blue, electric blue, shoulder, turquoise,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/hm/lcuuiw245a128d2164ec5929411650_600x600.jpg)

Made up of beautiful blues, this wall will make you feel like you’re under the sea!
Where to Find it: Right outside “The Seas with Nemo and Friends” in EPCOT.

## 12. The You Are Most Beautiful Wall

![text, font, happiness, fun, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/f8/x39bg7hy5a128d2132477717321371_564x553.jpg)

Every girl needs to get a picture with this wall as a reminder that she is beautiful!
Where to Find it: Harambe Market in Animal Kingdom.

## 13. The Blueberry Wall

![t shirt, clothing, shoulder, joint, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/xi/ncszeugy5a128d2224222422346725_600x800.jpg)

My personal favorite! I just love the colors and how happy and bright, but not overstated it is!
Where to Find in: EPCOT, right beneath Spaceship Earth.
