17 Apps to Help You Keep Your New Years Resolutions ...

Toshl Finance • ToDoIst • Concur • Remember the Milk • RE.minder • More ...

By Neecey

You need motivation and will power but you can also use apps to help keep your New Year’s resolutions. Need to Be fitter? Need to eat more healthy? Need to be more organized and manage your time better? Need to manage your money better? There’s an app for that. Apps like these:

1. Toshl Finance

Keep to your finances in order and follow through on your promises to cut your spending with this budgeting app. The app is free, but the most useful elements you have to pay for.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

2. ToDoIst

If you have a few big goals that you wish to accomplish in the New Year, then join the 5 million+ people that have used the ToDoIst app to create their to-do list. Keep track of the things you said you would do and see how far you have come in completing them.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

3. Concur

Put your financial goals on a more business footing with this app. You can check off the receipts with your budgets and see if your financial goals and your spending concur. It is handy for the more business-minded users.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

4. Remember the Milk

This is a very popular app that you can use for long-term planning and organization, or you can use it in the short term such as for your shopping list. Structure your planning with a list and you are more likely to see your promises through.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

5. RE.minder

Need a reminder of what exactly your new year’s resolution is? Then use this app. It is also good if you have set a plan with numerous steps that include deadlines. If you are being efficient about your goals, then this is the app for you.
IOS
itunes.apple.com

6. Expensify

This is an app that will allow you to keep a close check on the money you are spending. The people that have vowed to stop spending as much or to stick to a budget are going to benefit from this app.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

7. Errands to-do List

The great thing about the Errands To-Do List app is that it is very easy to learn and very basic, so you can pick it up and use it without having to navigate through too many categories. It is a great pick-up-and-go app.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

8. Mint Bills: Bill Pay & Money

You may have heard of the Mint app, this one deals specifically with your bills. If you are able to take a hold of your regular outgoings, you may be able to free up some money so you may afford to do your New Year’s resolution.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

9. 30/30

If you want to be more productive in the New Year, then this app may help you organize your time so you do not spend too much time being underproductive. It is good for helping you take set breaks and getting into good habits.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

10. Shoeboxed Receipt Tracker

This is a good app you can start using before the New Year, especially if you have a lot of ad-hoc expenses as you buy presents. You can continue the receipt and expense checking process in the New Year to make yourself more organized with your money.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

11. Way of Life

Track your good and bad habits and make a note of them. The hope is that you will either be shocked into realizing your habits consume more of your life than you thought. Plus, you may see how your habit patterns are changing over time.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

12. Sworkit – Circuit Training Workouts

Circuit training is a fantastic way of building your strength and cardio stamina in tandem. Even if you do not use this app to track your fitness resolutions, you should still consider circuit training if you want to be stronger in the New Year.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

13. Bloom

Use it to flex your creative muscles in the New Year, or use it to create motivating images and collages that helps remind you why you are doing something. The more personal--the better!
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

14. Whole Foods Market

With this app, you may create more healthy and tasty meals. Many people decide they are going to get healthy in the New Year, but do not know how to go about it. With this app, you have the healthy eating part nailed.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

15. 21Habit

I love this one. Based on the principle that it takes 21 days to make or break a habit. This is the best part: You can create your own 21 day challenge and deposit $21. For every day you are successful, you earn your dollar back. If you don't succeed, 21 Habit donates your dollar to charity.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

16. My Diet Diary

Sometimes you have to bite the bullet and be prepared to lose weight the hard way. If you are having trouble eating a balanced and healthy diet, then counting calories may be your best bet, and with this app you may do that.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

17. Juice

Track your sleep, your eating and your exercise. This is for people that are genuinely interested in how their eating, sleeping and exercise habits are affecting their fitness and their weight loss.
IOS
itunes.apple.com
Android
play.google.com

What New Year’s resolutions are you making? Have you found apps to help?
