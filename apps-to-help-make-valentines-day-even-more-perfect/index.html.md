# 8 Valentines Day Apps to Make the Day Even More Perfect ...

It’s always good to have a little assistance to make Valentine’s Day as dreamy as you want it to be. These days, helps comes in the form of some handy **Valentine’s Day apps**.

## 1. Shopular Coupons

_ play.google.com_
_itunes.apple.com_

This is one of the many free apps that offer coupons on various items throughout the year. The great thing about this app is the fact it offers extra discounts and coupons during holiday seasons, and one of their holiday seasons is Valentine’s Day. You can get your hands on a lot of discounts and vouchers for Valentines-related gifts, which is going to save you money on your purchases (so you can buy more).

## 2. Gravy

_ itunes.apple.com_

This app is a guide to the things happening in your area. If you are going out with your loved one either on Valentine’s Day or around that time, you should try this app because it can plug you into the local scene. Instead of doing the same old thing and letting the search engines decide what you do, you can use an app that is custom made for people that are yearning for a place to go.

## 3. IKamasutra Positions Guide

_ play.google.com_

The book this app is based on is greatly overhyped. The sexual stuff only really comes at the end (giggity), and most of it is useless because most people do not bend as if they are made of Playdoh, nor can they hold the weight of three people with their ankle. The app on the other hand, simply features the positions and the instructions you need, along with ratings such as how easy the position is and how you may improve the experience.

## 4. Movies by Flixter

![Movies by Flixter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/u9/ey15oud554d0f35435d42554905912_322x572.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/0F2xv3)

Going to the cinema is a pretty standard thing to do on Valentine’s Day but still, it is a good idea to know what is on at the cinema and when you should get there. It is always a good idea to pick the film before you arrive, or you will be one of those couples stood in the foyer arguing if they would watch Bridget Jones 7 Judgment Day or Die Hard 13 Just die already. This is one of the few Valentine’s Day apps that allows you to figure out what movie you want to see before you get to the theater.

## 5. Love Quotes

![Love Quotes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/oo/ycqk0c7k54d0f354ca17d519599734_320x512.jpg)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.skollabs.love&hl=en)

This app is full of pictures with sweet and romantic sayings on them that you can send to the one you love. You will have seen the images before on Facebook and Google images, but with the app you get a library of them so you can send the one that perfectly explains your feelings.

## 6. 1-800 Flowers

![1-800 Flowers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/be/xplbs46y54d0f354d9152922951198_288x512.jpg)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.flowers1800.androidapp2&hl=en)

For want of a better description, this is an app for sending emergency flowers. They send out flowers the same day in some locations. It plugs you into a network of florists. It is a nice enough app for people that remember in advance because it has plenty of choice, but is ideal for people that want to have flowers delivered the same day and happen to be in the right area.

## 7. Love Poem

_ play.google.com_

This app is full of poems you can use with your loved one. Even if he knows you didn’t write it, you can explain how it shows your true feelings. There are also plenty of romantic texts that you can lift from the app. It is not that you do not feel the feelings they describe, it is just that the app is better at putting them into words. Some of the poems may inspire you to write something of your own.

## 8. Open Table

![Open Table](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/bc/q2q9c0dh54d0f35652c51776586441_512x320.jpg)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opentable&hl=en)
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/opentable-restaurants-reservations/id296581815)

This is one of the most highly rated restaurant booking apps on the Google play marketplace at the moment. It has hundreds of restaurants registered with it. All you have to do is pick a restaurant and make a booking. It is just like calling the restaurant except you get to do it online via your app
