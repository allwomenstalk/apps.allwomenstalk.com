# 7 TV Channels You Can Stream Right on Your Smartphone ...

There are so many fantastic TV channels you can stream straight from your smartphone. It's a great option to have because one doesn't always have time to watch their favorite shows live! With these apps, you can watch all your shows on demand. Check out some of the best TV channels you can stream on the go!

## 1. ABC

![ABC](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/in/d5c1grhd_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/watch-abc/id364191819?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

With the free ABC app, you can watch the network live or on demand right on your smartphone! You can enjoy your favorite ABC shows, sports, local news, and weather! Sign in with your TV provider account to gain access to the app's content. I use this app ALL the time - I can't get enough of Modern Family, The Goldbergs, Nashville, etc! It's definitely one of the best TV channels you can stream on your smartphone.

## 2. ABC Family

![ABC Family](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ax/oc/p0una5kn_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/zrgs9k)

With the ABC Family app you can watch full episodes anytime, anywhere! You can choose from watching the show live or on demand. Just sign in with your TV provider and start watching! I love this free app because I'm always catching up on the latest Pretty Little Liars, Twisted, etc!

## 3. FOX

![FOX](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/g7/no21vhx3_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fox-now/id571096102?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

The FOX network has some of the best TV shows. With its free app, you can stream full-length episodes and view bonus content for select shows. You can follow along with the official show and cast Twitter streams and even join the conversation by sharing photos and clips via social media! This app definitely is worth the download!

## 4. HBO

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

With HBO GO, you can enjoy your favorite HBO shows, movies, comedy specials, documentaries, and more! The app gives you access to behind-the-scenes extras and has every episode of every season of the best of the network. It's free with your HBO subscription! You can watch new episodes of your favorite shows and movies simultaneously as they premiere on HBO! There are also streaming apps for Cinemax and Showtime!

## 5. NBC

![NBC](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/dh/ypnoamrp_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/nbc/id442839435?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

With the free NBC mobile app, you can watch full-length episodes of primetime, daytime, and late night favorites like The Blacklist, The Voice, etc. You can also keep track of your favorite shows and find out what's coming up with the network! You can create favorites, "follow" your favorite series, and share whatever you're watching via Facebook, Twitter, or email.

## 6. The Cw

![The Cw](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/p3/g8wi2cqo_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/1SWMgJ)

With the CW app, you can watch free full-length episodes of your favorite shows! You can even sign up for the app's new push notifications to stay connected to your favorite shows. You can stream your favorite shows like Arrow (my personal favorite), The Vampire Diaries, Supernatural, etc. You can also watch extended videos, previews for upcoming episodes, and behind-the-scenes extras and cast interviews! The app also offers the network's schedule so you can set reminders for new episodes!

## 7. A&E

![A&E](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/qj/pl0avb73_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/a-e/id571711580?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

With the A&E app, you can watch your favorite shows wherever and whenever you want! The app gives you access to exclusive A&E clips and full-length episodes! New content is added all the time and you can even create a customized watch list of all your favorite shows. The app is free to use - Comcast & DirectTV subscribers can log in and get access to the network's content. How awesome would it be to watch Duck Dynasty, Storage Wars, Bates Motel, and more on the go?

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many different TV networks that you can stream right on your smartphone. There are tons more but these are just some of my personal favorites. These apps will save you whenever you're bored! Which TV network has your favorite app/TV shows?
