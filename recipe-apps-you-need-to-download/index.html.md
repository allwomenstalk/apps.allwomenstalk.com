# 9 Recipe Apps You Need to Download if You Love to Cook ...

So, I've been trying my hand at cooking all kinds of different things, and one of the most important things that I do when I'm cooking is look for recipe apps that actually make sense for me to have! Recipe apps are something that I download constantly and that I am constantly on the look-out for. I love trying new recipes and these apps make it easy to find them. So, unleash the cook inside yourself!

## 1. Cook's Illustrated

_ itunes.apple.com_

By far, this is one of the best recipe apps on the market right now. This is actually a foolproof recipe app that will allow you to cook anything and everything! Do you want to try a new tomato sauce? Want to try cooking a steak a new way? Download this incredible app and try your hand at thousands of recipes!

## 2. Allrecipes.com Dinner Spinner

![Allrecipes.com Dinner Spinner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/07/2_allrecipes-com-dinner-spinner_320x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/allrecipes-com-dinner-spinner/id299515267)

Now, this is actually a pretty cool app that will give you a recipe simply by shaking up your phone! All you need to do is scan in any ingredient that you might have on hand, spin and the app will come up with a recipe that is not only delicious, but also totally unique!

## 3. Whole Foods Market Recipes

![Whole Foods Market Recipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/07/3_whole-foods-market-recipes_270x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/whole-foods-market-recipes/id320029256)

Of course we know that Whole Foods has everything in the world in terms of food, but did you know that this particular app has all kinds of recipes too? It has personal notation for recipes and store sales flyers for the Whole Foods closest to you, as well as recipe ratings and comments. It's a great and handy little app!

## 4. How to Cook Everything

_ itunes.apple.com_

If you are new to cooking or you just want to brush up on your skills in cooking, this is the recipe app for you! It's got over 2,000 recipes tucked away inside of it, along with 400 how-to illustrations, which makes it so, so easy. This is perfect for the chef that is on the go!

## 5. Ratio

_ itunes.apple.com_

Do you have a bunch of ingredients on hand, but have absolutely no idea what to do with them or how you should measure them out so that you can make something amazing? Well, Ratio does it for you! You can just scan everything in and it will come up with the ratios that you need to measure out.

## 6. BigOven

![BigOven](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/apps/2013/07/6_bigoven_270x480.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/zrysWG)

Now, this is one of those apps that is a must-have for anyone that is really looking to expand their culinary skills. You can find recipes for leftovers, can really figure out metrics and you can actually keep track of any and all recipes right inside the app!

## 7. Jamie Oliver's 20 Minute Meals

_ itunes.apple.com_

If you are on the run all of the time and you are looking for an app that will allow you to make something delish in about 20 minutes, this is the app for you! This app has tons of recipes that are amazingly easy to make – and super quick! Give it a try!

## 8. Simply Organic Recipes

_ itunes.apple.com_

What about the organic recipes? Wouldn't you want to make something that is completely and totally organic and healthy? This is the app for you! It's all about organic things and how to really stay healthy and make healthy, delicious dishes.

## 9. Weber's on the Grill

_ itunes.apple.com_

If you are all about the meat, all about the grill and all about perfection, this is the app for you! This app has saved me so many times when I had absolutely no idea how to make steak or potatoes on the grill. If you want to be the ultimate grill-master, this app is for you!

While these are just a few of the awesome recipes apps out there, they are some of my favorites. Do you have any favorite recipe apps that you want to share? Give it up!
