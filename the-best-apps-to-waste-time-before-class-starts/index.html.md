# Best Apps for Those Who Want to Waste Time  before or during  Class ...

Don't you hate when you get to class extra early and have no one to talk to? Well, with the help of technology, you don't have to sit around feeling awkward. You can pull out your phone to text your friends, look up gossip on the internet, or play some fun games. If you haven't downloaded any entertaining apps recently, here are a few that are great time wasters:

## 1. Cute or Not

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_

If you're grumpy about the fact that you had to wake up early for class, this app will change your entire mood. It allows you to look at hundreds of adorable pictures of dogs and cats. When you look through them, you can swipe right if you think the pet is cute and swipe left if you're not impressed by the picture. You can even upload your own photo of your pet to see how popular they become.

## 2. Cube Jump

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_

This is a mindless game that can become pretty addictive. All you have to do is tap the screen to make a cube jump from one platform to the other without falling into the black abyss. Just make sure to turn off your phone's sound, because the game can get pretty loud.

## 3. Agar.io

![Agar.io](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/rn/c79x7f9r55d480c2b2a1b952227952_600x338.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/agar-agar/id992479750)

This game lets you play against other people in the world. You start out as a small dot on the screen that you're able to move around the board. Your goal is to eat as many little dots as you can to grow bigger. You just have to avoid the large dots that can eat you right up and make you lose the game.

## 4. Unseen

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_

You don't have to play games on your phone while you're waiting for class to begin. You can log onto a social media site in order to stay social. Instead of using your regular sites, like Facebook and Instagram, you can try making an account on Unseen, which is built for students to interact with one another.

## 5. Puzzle 11

![Puzzle 11](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y1/m9/zg3xwllx55d480c11071c810956675_322x484.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/1MXLvg)

If you're waiting for a math class to start, then you might as well get in a little practice before your teacher shows up. This app allows you to play puzzle games that test your ability to count and to think ahead. You definitely need your head in the game in order to win.

## 6. YouCam Makeup

![YouCam Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xx/as/vop4n1do55d480c1b8e52501235121_322x527.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/youcam-makeup-virtual-makeover/id863844475)

Do you try to plan out your outfit for the next day when you're bored in class? If you do, you'll love this app, which lets you give yourself a makeover on your phone. If you want to be productive and come up with new ways to wear your eyeliner and blush, then this app will help.

## 7. Episode

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_

This game is lighthearted and fun. It contains dozens of different "stories" that you can choose to read. Once you do, you'll be presented with several options. You can choose things like your character's name, what they say to their friends, and who they want to date. Once you try it, you'll be addicted to it.

There's no reason to be bored in this day and age. When you're waiting for your teacher to enter the room, you might as well enjoy yourself by playing with these fun phone applications. What apps do you usually use right before class starts?
