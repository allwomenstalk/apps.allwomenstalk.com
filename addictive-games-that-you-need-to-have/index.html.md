# 7 Addictive Games That You Need to Have ...

There are so many addictive games that you need to have on your phone! Granted, these games will probably consume your life. But when has that ever been a bad thing? I rarely get tangled in the whole 'addicted to a game' web, but when I do, I play that game until I physically cannot anymore. Check out some of the addictive games I've been hooked on lately. Feel free to comment with the name of the game YOU'VE been playing nonstop!

## 1. 2048

![2048](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kp/r7/s1qfpuf4_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/2048/id840919914?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
This game sounds simple enough, but it's so incredibly frustrating, which makes it so incredibly addicting. Crazy how that works, right? All you have to do is join the numbers on the tiles together to get to the 2048 tile. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. When you get to the 2048 tile, you reach a new high score! Simple enough? Download it and try it out! This is just one of the addictive games you need to have on your phone!

## 2. Heads up!

![Heads up!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/h5/tplx0zci_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/heads-up/id623592465?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $0.99
This game is seriously one of the funnest games to play with a group of friends! You choose a category - ranging from naming celebrities, singing some tunes, doing silly accents and more - and then hold the phone up to your forehead. The other person needs to try their best to help you figure out whatever word/name/object is currently showing up on the screen. The app provides fun tips and hints to make the game a bit more challenging! Once you start, you'll play all night.

## 3. Piano Tiles

![Piano Tiles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/k1/vdzb6rmw_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/piano-tiles/id848160327?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
The rules to this game are VERY simple. Still, it won't leave you from wanting to pull your hair out every time you play it. All you have to do is avoid touching the white tile. The game's slogan? "Everyone can handle it, but not everyone can handle it well." That's already intimidating enough for me. Pick one of the five speed modes and try it out!

## 4. Match the Dots

![Match the Dots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ju/uq/xbuc4v10_320x416.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/match-the-dots/id690169259?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
I don't know about you, but I get hooked on games with timers. It pushes me to want to beat the level before my time is up! With this game, al you have to do is match the dots in your allotted time. For bonus points, try connecting BIG groups of dots! The game offers 100+ completely free levels.

## 5. The Sims Free Play

![The Sims Free Play](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/vd/uwguh7zo_568x320.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/the-sims-freeplay/id466965151?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Honestly, it's crazy just how many people are addicted to Sims! When I say 'addicted', I mean. Some people will lose sleep over this game. There are tons of different versions of the game that you can play. With the 'free play' version, you can live out your perfect Sims story all for free!

## 6. Family Feud

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
I have to admit, I am hooked on Family Feud. I just think it's such a fun way to challenge your mind! The categories are also really fun and on this app, you can challenge your friends. It's just like being on the actual show! It might not be popular with everyone, but it's definitely addictive.

## 7. 7 Little Words

![7 Little Words](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/os/g6l6ume9_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/7-little-words/id431434152?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
This game is actually pretty old, but it's so good! Each bite-size puzzle consists of 7 clues, 7 mystery words, and 20 letter groups. You have to find the mystery word by deciphering the clues! It's definitely a game that'll challenge your brain. You'll feel frustrated but very determined to finish it.

Well, there you have it! These are just some of the games I've been hooked on. Feel free to share the names of the games you've been playing. Fair warning - I'm blaming you if I can't quit playing them! Which games have been keeping you up at night?
