# How to Play Pokemon Go \- Your Complete Guide  ...

So now you're one of millions who has downloaded the Pokemon Go app and are now trolling your town to catch things and have fun. You probably get the basics of the game and that's pretty fun. Did you know that there are lots of resources out there to help you maximize the fun? Here are some websites to help you get the most out of it.

## 1. PokeVision.com

Here's a website that can help you find invisible guys. Use the site as a way to grab Eevees that you couldn't otherwise see. What could be more cool than that? Simply type your location into the website and it will tell you where to go. You'll get the time and exact location so it's easy to go right where you need to go.

## 2. Pokemon Type Chart

If you need help figuring out how to beat others, this handy chart will be your new best friend. Simply download it to your device so that you can refer to it anytime you go into gyms. You can find the chart here: [dnd-wiki.org](https://dnd-wiki.org/wiki/Main_Page)

## 3. Pokemon Hatching Chart

![Hamamatsuchō Station, Pokemon Go, energy drink, red bull, Resource,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/do/n5h2lps85791b6ee1380f701583262_600x338.jpg)

Head to [imgur.com](https://imgur.com/gallery/Q29cL) and you can get a good idea of what your eggs might hatch into based on whether they are 2km, 5km or 10km. Now when you get an egg you won't have to worry so much about what you're going to get out if it. Cool, right?

## 4. PogoToolkit.com

If you need help with the evolution of your Pokemon, this site is exactly what you've been looking for. Use the drop down menu to enter your Pokemon and its CP. You then get the CP of the evolution with the multiplier next to it. Isn't that awesome?

## 5. TheSilphRoad.com

If you love stats and want to follow them on Pokemon Go, this is the website for you. In addition to being able to read through the stats of all of the Pokemon on Pokemon Go, you also get a list of ways to gain XP and valuable information on the first 40 trainer levels. When numbers makes the game more fun, this site should satisfy your hopes and dreams.

## 6. PidgeyCalc.com

If you want step by step directions on how to evolve your Pokemons, you absolutely need this website. It will tell you exactly the number of candies you need to evolve a Pokemon and how to get the max XP out of all of your Pokemons.

## 7. RankedBoost.com/pokemon-go/

There is a ton of good stuff on this site so you definitely want to check it out today. There are lists on how much CP you get when you level up using candies and stardust. You also get definitions on footsteps, the definition of levels and which guys are the best ones to catch. You also get a guide on how to find Pokemons and where the best places to catch them are. Yay!
