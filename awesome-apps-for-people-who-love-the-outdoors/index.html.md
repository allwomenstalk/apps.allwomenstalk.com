# 7 Awesome Apps for People Who Love the Outdoors ...

There are so many great apps for outdoors lovers. I've recently gotten into hiking and these apps have definitely come in handy! You can track the weather, find hiking paths, look at maps, and so much more. These apps for outdoors lovers will definitely motivate you to get out there and embrace mother nature!

## 1. Motion X Gps

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $1.99

Are you an avid hiker? If so, this app would be perfect for you. It offers topographical maps so you'll always know your way around. You can also opt to download the maps ahead of time, just in case your service isn't so reliable during your hike. You can track your journey, use a trusty compass to find your way around, and save your waypoints. This is just one of the many apps for outdoors lovers!

## 2. Trip Journal

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $2.99

This app is perfect for documenting your trip. You can capture photos and take notes tied to different waypoints to create a "rich, Indiana Jones-style journal." How awesome does that sound? This app helps you organize your photos - making it a great way to remember all the details. You can even share them with your fellow hikers.

## 3. Park Maps

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $0.99

This app offers maps of over 250 U.S. National parks, monuments, and recreational areas. You can view the maps in landscape or portrait mode, whatever is easier for you. The maps are downloaded straight to your phone so no internet connection is needed once they're downloaded. Some of the maps include Grand Canyon, Yellowstone, Yosemite, and many more. It's great for hiking, camping, and RV road trips!

## 4. All Trails

![All Trails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x5/li/ucnprauw_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/alltrails-hiking-mountain/id405075943?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free

Looking for a place near home to hike, bike, or run? This app has you covered! You'll be surprised by how much nature actually surrounds you. You can browse through guides for 50,000 trails across North America complete with photos, reviews, and tracks for different outdoor activities. You can track your activities and add photos to easily share your adventures with friends! If you know of a trail that's not included in the app, you can add new trails to the database!

## 5. Accuweather

![Accuweather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cq/b2/cb24mv5u_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/accuweather-weather-for-life/id300048137?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free

Stay in the loop about the latest weather with this app! The leading minute-by-minute weather forecast is hyper-localized to your exact street address. It'll send you severe weather alerts straight to your phone to keep you clear from the storms! The app has an animated weather radar and maps for tracking the storm's path. With this app, the weather will never take you by surprise!

## 6. Geocaching

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $9.99

Ready to go on a modern day treasure hunt? Players hide caches of small items and tokens and then post the GPS coordinates for other players to find. There are more than 2 million active geocaches ongoing worldwide! The app provides a list of nearby caches to go hunt for! This is a great way to get a group of friends involved and out and about!

## 7. Star Chart

![Star Chart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/ha/ufzh7fmg_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/star-chart/id345542655?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free

With this app, you can look through the eyes of your phone to see a "high-tech window into the whole visible universe." All you have to do is point your device at the sky and Star Chart will tell you exactly what you're looking at!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome apps for outdoors lovers. With these, you can find the nature around you and make the time to go appreciate it. What's your favorite app to use for the outdoors?
