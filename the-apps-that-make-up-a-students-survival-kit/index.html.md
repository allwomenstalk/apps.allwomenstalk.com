# The Apps Every Student Needs in Their School Survival Kit ...

For help with your studies and just getting to grip with life as a student, there are thousands of apps. They help you get organized, aid your work, keep track of your money and also keep you informed and entertained. Every student is obviously different and will want a unique selection of apps, but here’s my selection:

## 1. Microsoft Word

![text, font, mobile device, area, View,,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/l6/ngvv4okw575ab7c005af8604367438_500x572.jpg)

The absolute daddy when it comes to word processing programs, and this stripped back free version is a godsend for students!

Download at:
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.office.word&hl=en_GB)

## 2. OneDrive

![text, mobile phone, font, mobile device, gadget,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tr/i4/mcxmavza575ab7c01c56e637376482_500x572.jpg)

This is a wonderful cloud service from Microsoft that lets you access all of your files from multiple devices, which means that you will never get caught out if you leave your laptop or your phone at home.

Download at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/microsoft-onedrive-cloud-storage/id477537958)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.skydrive&hl=en_GB)

## 3. Groupon

![dish, food, produce, meal, document,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y0/y9/mmizx9u7575ab7c0735b8086938937_500x572.jpg)

Living the student life can be tough, but Groupon makes it a little bit easier by showcasing all of the best deals and discounts of the day/week/month!

Download at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/groupon-deals-coupons-shopping/id352683833)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.groupon&hl=en_us)

## 4. Accuweather

![AccuWeather, text, mobile phone, gadget, mobile device,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/dd/flg7nlht575ab7c09ed0d052763874_500x572.jpg)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.accuweather.android&hl=en_us)
