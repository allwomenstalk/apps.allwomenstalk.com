# 7 Awesome Apps for Movie Lovers ...

There are tons of amazing apps for movie lovers! These all come in handy for me because I'm ALWAYS watching movies! I love keeping up with release dates, cast interviews, and trailers. All these apps help me do just that! Check out the awesome apps for movie lovers.

## 1. Imdb

![Imdb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iw/dh/dt/s1efi538_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/tvARi5)

IMDb is definitely one of the best apps for movie lovers. I'm constantly looking things up! Whether it be upcoming projects or movie cast members, this app always has the answer! You can find showtimes, watch trailers, browse photos, and track your must-see list! You can rate your favorite movies. IMDb has the largest collection of movie and celebrity info! For every movie, they list who made it, the plot, ratings, trailers, photos, reviews, quotes, and much more! All this for FREE? It's fantastic!

## 2. Itunes Trailer

![Itunes Trailer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/qy/gq/b3nu4bhd_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/UeEfPH)

I'm ALWAYS on this app! It puts the newest and most exclusive movie previews right in your hands! You can browse different trailers, clips, and featurettes! I love this free app because it shows you the trailers of not only the biggest Hollywood blockbusters, but also independent cinema! It even offers a year-long calendar of movie releases, so you'll never be out of the loop!

## 3. Netflix

![Netflix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/v6/ps/egyzpi1l_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/netflix/id363590051?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

The app is free to download. However, you must actually have a Netflix account to be able to stream it! I don't think I need to go on and on about why the Netflix app is so great! It's world's largest subscription service for watching movies on your phone! You can add movies to your "list" to make sure you get back to it eventually. The app also offers personalized recommendations based on the movies you've watched! I don't know what I'd do without Netflix!

## 4. Fandango Movies

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fandango-movies-times-tickets/id307906541?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

If you're a constant movie-goer, then the Fandango movie app is perfect for you! You can check out the latest showtimes, buy tickets through the app, and check out exclusive Fandango content! You can stream high-quality trailers, exclusive interviews, and cast photos! The Fandango app will even find the theaters near you and send you showtimes and ratings! A lot of the times, you'll find exclusive deals on the free app so it's definitely worth checking out.

## 5. Hulu plus

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hulu-plus/id376510438?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

Like Netflix, Hulu Plus is free to download if you already have a paid subscription! On it, you can watch the full current seasons of the most popular TV shows! If you're a movie lover, chances are you love TV shows too. So, this app is perfect for that! Stream your favorite shows and never be behind on new episodes!

## 6. Redbox Instant

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

With the Redbox Instant app, you can get your discs plus digital! You can even stream movies instantly. It lets you reserve the newest releases at your local Redbox, or download movies you purchased on the web to watch offline! The app is totally convenient and keeps things affordable!

## 7. HBO GO

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

With a subscription to the channel, you can stream movies and TV shows straight from your HBO Go app! It's seriously one of my favorite apps because I can catch up on Girls OR binge-watch Entourage all over again. It's definitely a must-have for movie lovers because they always have the best ones! P.S. other channels like Showtime and Cinemax also have these apps!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome apps for movie lovers! Which app do you always use? Which one should every movie-lover download?
