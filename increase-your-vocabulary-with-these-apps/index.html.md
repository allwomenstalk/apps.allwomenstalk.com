# 7 Apps for People Who Want to Sound Smarter 🤓 and Increase Their Vocab ...

The more words you know, the more intelligent you'll sound. An expansive vocabulary can help you out on job interviews, on dates, and during arguments. That's why you should aim to learn as much of the English language as you possible can. If you're interested in the idea, here are a few different apps that will help you increase your vocabulary:

## 1. Vocabulary Builder

![Vocabulary Builder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/c5/cjpoqqx955def5811f022112731790_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/vocabulary-builder-from-magoosh/id890252250?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This app helps you learn new words in the easiest way possible. However, it might make you feel like you're taking a test. It will present you with a word and then ask you to choose the correct definition of it. If you get it wrong, then that word will pop up again later in the game to make sure that you've actually learned the definition.

## 2. 7 Little Words

![7 Little Words](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/wr/girmfn0d55def58129cee189407580_322x572.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/7-little-words/id431434152?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This game is so much fun that you won't even realize you're learning while you're playing it. It will present you with a list of 7 definitions and then ask you to create words that fit those definitions from a group of letters at the bottom of your screen. Even if you don't know the answer, clicking on different combinations of letters will eventually help you win the game.

## 3. Word to Word

![Word to Word](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/mp/i0i5o2wd55def5833bda2395027646_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/word-to-word-fun-addictive/id441894920?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This is a matching game with a twist. It will ask you to match words from the left side of the screen to words on the right side of the screen. Those words won't always be synonyms, though. Sometimes, they'll just have something in common, like "bullet" and "handgun."

## 4. Quizzitive

![Quizzitive](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/pw/qhhg6tlp55def5818063b752382017_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/quizzitive-merriam-webster/id645814720?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This app has a few tiny games inside of it. You might be asked to guess the definition of a certain word, or you might be asked to pick a word to describe a particular picture. Of course, no matter what question you're asked, this application will help your brain learn new words that you've never been met with before.

## 5. Visual Vocab SAT

![Visual Vocab SAT](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/ke/tqxywpms55def581afc2c946220893_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/visual-vocab-sat-free/id677336839?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

You don't actually have to be studying for the SAT in order to use this helpful app. Of course, if you are going to be taking the test this year, then it's a great idea to download it for practice. It will show you different pictures of people and objects with the definitions beneath them. If you're a visual learner, then you'll benefit from this application.

## 6. The Word of the Day

_ On iTunes: itunes.apple.com_

You don't want to stuff your brain with too much information at once. That's why it's a great idea to download this app, which will give you a new word every single day. As long as you remember to check it, then you'll learn at least thirty new words every single month!

## 7. Wordbrain

![Wordbrain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/g8/x99j8q7l55def582dc990113956150_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wordbrain/id708600202?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This game will give you a board with different letters on it. All you have to do is create words with those letters. It sounds simple, but the longer you play, the more challenging the rounds become.

It's important to learn as many different words as your brain can handle. After all, the better you become at speaking, the better you'll be at writing essays and acing job interviews. What other apps have you used that have helped you learn new words?
