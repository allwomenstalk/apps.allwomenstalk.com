# The App That Makes It Easier to Apply for College Scholarships ...

Your last years of high school are stressful ones, because you're forced to prepare for college. That can be difficult, which is why you'll need all the help you can get. According to [Teen Vogue,](https://www.teenvogue.com/story/scholly-college-scholarship-app) [myscholly.com](https://myscholly.com/) is the new app that'll help you find college scholarships, so you can save money on tuition.

How does it work? "Scholly uses an eight-parameter system to filter the database of scholarships Christopher created. Those eight parameters are the eight most common eligibility requirements for scholarships – gender, location, career aspiration, ethnicity, etc. When you sign up, you answer those eight questions, and Scholly gives you a list of scholarship options."
