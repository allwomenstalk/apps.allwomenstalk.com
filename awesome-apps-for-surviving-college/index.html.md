# 7 Awesome Apps for Surviving College ...

Thankfully for all of us, there are tons of apps for surviving college. I know, there’s an app for pretty much anything nowadays! When it’s not busy being the best time of your life, college can also be insanely stressful. There’s schoolwork, extracurricular activities, maintaining a social life, etc. How does everyone do it?! If you need some help, check out these apps for surviving college. I’m sure they’ll be of great use to you!

## 1. BenchPrep

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
BenchPrep is definitely one of the best apps for surviving college. It offers test prep courses for GRE, GMAT, LSAT, MCAT, ACT, and 50+ more tests. Just reading those test names already makes me cringe! But the apps platform is adaptive, fun and effortless! The app includes hundreds of practice questions, flashcards, in-app purchases, and almost 600 study lessons. Go ahead and start picking your brain!

## 2. IStudiez Pro

![IStudiez Pro](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/g2/0y/lzacxky9_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/istudiez-pro/id310636441?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $2.99
If you’re hopeless at remembering deadlines, this app is for you! With iStudiez Pro, you can track tasks/deadlines, plan homework and arrange assignments! It’s an awesome tool to arrange your schedule and help you stick to it. The app lets you know if you have any pending tasks for your upcoming classes and events. The app even offers the options to back up your data by sending it to your email address. It’s definitely worth the price!

## 3. EasyBib

![EasyBib](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sg/x5/ea/ydjqownc_360x480.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/uweFjV)
**Price:** Free
With this app, you can create accurate MLA, APA, and Chicago style citations in seconds! All you need to do is scan a book barcode or type in the name of a book. You can build and manage your works cited list and once you’re done, export your citations to EasyBib.com! With this, you have no more excuses for putting off that research paper you need to turn in!

## 4. Dictionary.com

![Dictionary.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/xz/t6/f5zbgz3v_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dictionary-com-dictionary/id308750436?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
You’d be surprised at how often you’ll be using this app. It’s so convenient to have the dictionary app because you no longer have to get stuck on any major reading assignments. This app works offline, which means you need no Internet connection when searching words! That’ll come in handy when your dorm room doesn’t have that Wi-Fi hotspot!

## 5. Skype

![Skype](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/p6/op/ltpizufw_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/USsN9G)
**Price:** Free
College usually means being away from home. That means the only way you can have some face-to-face time with friends and family is through Skype! It doesn’t seem like it’s so important, but Skype will save you from some major homesickness. Get your friends and family to download the app and make time to catch up with everyone! It’ll make you feel more connected to them!

## 6. TED

![TED](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/bz/fb/m300sqil_319x543.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ted/id376183339?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
The TED app presents talks from some of the world’s most fascinating people! You can find over 1,400 TEDTalk videos (more added each week) from education radicals, tech geniuses, medical mavericks, music legends and more! You can browse the entire library at any time and watch the videos with subtitles in over 90 languages. The app updates almost everyday with new talks - it’s the perfect way to stay in the loop with everything going on!

## 7. StudyBlue

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
With the StudyBlue app, you’ll learn how to study better and learn faster! You can make, study, and share mobile flashcards, study guides, and quizzes! It could also be a fun way to study with your friends!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome apps that’ll help you survive college. Which one can’t you live without?!
