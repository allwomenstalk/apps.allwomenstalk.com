# This Epic SmartPhone Case Prints Your Photos Right on the Spot ...

No, you're not dreaming. Yes, you did read that right. The Prynt case turns your phone into a polaroid camera by allowing you to snap a photo and then instantly print it! The co-founders of the case chatted with [techcrunch.com](https://techcrunch.com/2014/11/13/the-prynt-case-turns-your-smartphone-into-a-polaroid-camera/) about the prototype and, if we're being honest, I CAN'T WAIT FOR THIS.

The printing process takes a little less than a minute and holds one sheet of photo paper. The goal, however, is to launch a consumer-ready version that can support up to 30 sheets, each with a 30 second print time. When you run out of photo paper, you can easily order more paper right from the app at just 30 cents per sheet. How amazing is that?! Currently, the prototype is compatible with smartphones that have a 4-inch screen. Don't worry iPhone 6 Plus/Galaxy Note users, there ARE plans to launch a version that will support your devices, too. When it launches, Prynt's app will be available for both iOS and Android users.

So, if you're thinking, "WHEN CAN I GET THIS?!," the answer is, in the near future. Prynt is expected to start selling the device for a price of $99 through a Kickstarter campaign. The campaign will most likely start in January 2015 but it's unknown when the device will actually start shipping.
