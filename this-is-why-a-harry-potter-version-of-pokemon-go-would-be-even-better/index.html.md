# This is Why a Harry Potter Version of Pokmon Go Would Be Even Better ...

As hugely popular as Pokémon Go is, can you imagine how incredible the Harry Potter Go game would be? Here are a few of the reasons HP Go would be even better than Pokémon Go (I'm looking at you, Rowling).

## 1. The Professor

![hair, person, facial hair, photograph, painting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/rb/w5yjzvqg5790bd83e4e8e570286230_514x729.jpg)

Sure, Professor Willow from Pokémon Go is kind of ... well, hot ... as animated game characters go, but can you imagine being guided by Professor Dumbledore instead? Or maybe McGonagall? Or even Snape?

## 2. Get Sorted

![painting, person, photograph, photography, art,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/aj/ltnkknv95790bdf5d814a029826215_500x600.jpg)

Rather than choosing a team - Valor, Mystic, or Instinct - in Harry Potter Go, you'd be sorted into the four houses - Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff, or Slytherin - by the Sorting Hat.

## 3. Magical Items

![mobile device, book, HARRY, POTTER, AND,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g5/n9/tnapmtno5790be8917b16635403968_600x914.jpg)

Instead of your run-of-the-mill incense, lures, and raspberries in Pokémon Go, imagine the cool Potions you could make as even a first-year wizard at Hogwarts!

## 4. Quidditch

![antique, major appliance,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/jw/pr9zsxa85790beb51c8a1327175086_600x842.jpg)

I'm not sure exactly how the designers would work this in, but I've been dying to try my hand at Quidditch (I think I'd make a great Seeker), and I'd love to see it somehow included in HP Go.

## 5. Gotta Catch 'Em All!

![animal, ratite, wildlife, vertebrate, stallion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sy/bt/fdphrdv75790c033537b4524848869_564x624.jpg)

Can you imagine how cool it would be to catch some of the Fantastic Beasts instead of all those low-level Weedles, Pidgeys, and Rattatas?

## 6. Level up = OWLS and NEWTS

![owl, animal, bird, falcon, bird of prey,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/fn/l4c79kdw5790c06552e41099789845_600x807.jpg)

Rather than simply leveling up, like you do in Pokémon Go, you could work your way through the seven years at Hogwarts, passing your OWLS and NEWTS... that would be even more exciting!

## 7. On the down Side...

![person, hair, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bo/ss/i5jhooxt5790c0e21c4b8756560302_500x600.jpg)

The only down side I can think of to a Harry Potter Go game would be... honestly... that I would never, ever be a productive member of society again. Truly. How would any of us get anything else done?

Can you think of any other reasons why Harry Potter Go would kick Pokémon Go's butt?
