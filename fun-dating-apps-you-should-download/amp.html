7 Fun Dating Apps You Should Download ...

Tinder • HowAboutWe • OKCupid • POF (plenty of Fish) • CMB (Coffee Meets Bagel) • More ...

By Vanessa

If you're in need of some fun dating apps, look no further! There are so many different apps out there that can help you find the relationship you're looking for. It's no surprise that social media is now the most convenient way to meet your soulmate. Who would've thought? Although there are SO many fun dating apps out there, these are the ones you should be checking out.

1. Tinder

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

Tinder is one of best dating apps out there right now. It's an awesome way to meet new people that are nearby! Tinder shows you someone nearby it thinks you should know, and lets you anonymously 'like' or 'pass' on them. If someone you like happens to like you back, Tinder allows you to send them a message! It does require Facebook to login but does not share anything on your profile! It's definitely one of the fun dating apps you should download!

2. HowAboutWe

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

HowAboutWe is really easy and fun to use! You post a date idea ("How About We...grab some drinks after work") or whatever you'd like, and people nearby will be able to see it. If someone's interested in your date idea, they'll message you! You can view ideas from singles near you who share your interests! You can browse photos, and profiles, and set up your date!

3. OKCupid

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

Dating has never been so fun, simple, or free! With OKCupid, you can meet and chat with new people. You're able to broadcast your date suggestions and find people with matching interests. Although the app is free, it does offer special "packages" where you can pay to become an 'A-list member". As an A-lister, you get a lot more features!

4. POF (plenty of Fish)

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

With over 55 million members, POF is one of the world's largest dating sites! With the POF app, you can create a free account, chat, flirt, and exchange messages with local singles! You get instant access to sending/receiving messages, uploading images, and more! You even get to discover who viewed your profile and who wants to meet you! POF also offers upgraded memberships.

5. CMB (Coffee Meets Bagel)

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

CMB is fairly new and already getting rave reviews! The app has been talked about on the Today Show, NYTimes, Marie Claire, and more! Every day at noon, Coffee Meets Bagel introduces you to one single with whom you share mutual friends on Facebook! You then have 24 hours to 'like' or 'pass' your match. Mutual likes lead you to a direct connection via text message on a dedicated phone line. The app will keep your personal phone number hidden! That is, until you choose to reveal it to your app. They never post anything on your Facebook profile so don't worry about that!

6. Skout

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

Skout is a great network for meeting new people! You instantly meet people near you or around the world! Skout allows you to meet people by preference and proximity. You can chat, see who checked you out, get updates from nearby users, and more! Privacy is taken very seriously and your private information will never be given out!

7. Zoosk

For iPhone: itunes.apple.com

Zoosk uses its behavioral matchmaking engine to set you up with your perfect match! You can browse millions of singles for free, see who's viewed you, and show interest in other singles! By subscribing, you can enjoy advanced features!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many fun dating apps that you should totally look into. Every app offers something different. Who knows, you might get on one of these apps with no intentions and end up finding your soulmate! Which app are you going to download?
