# 7 Apps to Help Organize Your Life ...

There are so many fantastic apps to help organize your life. Whether you need to organize your thoughts, shopping list, photos, etc., these apps will help you! Life can be so stressful but it does't need to be! Live clutter-free with these apps to help organize your life!

## 1. Dropbox

![Dropbox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ra/ap/b5u3ch2z_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dropbox/id327630330?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
With the Dropbox app, you can organize all your photos, docs, and videos anywhere and even share them with ease! You can access any file you save to your Dropbox from all your computers, iPhones/iPads, and from the web. When you sign up, you get 2 GB of space - for free! You can share a link to your biggest files, instead of having to attach them to your emails! It offers great convenience and is definitely one of the best apps to help organize your life!

## 2. Clear

![Clear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/i0/dmgh318y_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/clear-tasks-to-do-list/id493136154?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $4.99
This app might not be free, but it's definitely worth the download! The app has a beautiful and elegant interface and is easy and quick for busy people! On the app you can create all different sorts of lists. You can try a personal to-do list, shopping list, guest list, bucket list, etc. With this app you'll simplify your life by organizing everything in just one place!

## 3. Lifelock Wallet

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
What could be more convenient than having your entire wallet in your phone? The LifeLock Wallet app brings you a "mobile" wallet and on-the-go access to the most comprehensive identity theft protection. On this free app, you can store information from all the cards in your wallet -- ID, membership, gift, and more. In the event of wallet theft or loss, the app will help you cancel your cards!

## 4. Grocery Pal

![Grocery Pal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h4/fm/urgtk806_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/grocery-pal-in-store-weekly/id339684652?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
This app will point you toward weekly sales at your local supermarkets and discount stores! On the Grocery Pal app, you'll find savings from stores such as CVS, Walmart, RiteAid,Target, Kmart, Dollar General, and many, many more! You can even add items to your shopping list right from the app! You can redeem coupons in-store and create as many lists as you want! It's an awesome way to keep your grocery needs in order!

## 5. Check

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
Check is one of the best apps that'll help you stay on top of your bills and finances. You'll never miss a bill or get hit with overdraft and late fees again! When bills are due or funds are low, the app will let you know. That way, you'll never be caught off guard! It's definitely a neat way to keep your finances in order and organized.

## 6. Flipboard

![Flipboard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ak/dx/vw79fsvp_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/flipboard-your-social-news/id358801284?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Pricce:** Free
The Flipboard app is like your own personal magazine! On it, you can catch up on the news, read stories from around the world and browse articles, videos, and photos that friends are sharing. All you need to do is pick a few interests and then tap any of the titles to begin flipping through them all. You can save the things that you find by tapping the "+" button to collect your items. It's an awesome way to keep all your different interests organized in one place!

## 7. Tripit

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $0.99
With the TripIt app, you can organize your travel plans all in one place! All you need to do is forward your travel confirmation emails to plans@tripit.com and the app will automatically create a detailed daily itinerary for every trip! You can also add or edit plans manually - from the app or on their website. You can even share your travel plans via email or social media to your friends!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of MANY apps that help you organize your life. These apps are all worth downloading - they'll all come in handy at one point or another! What's your favorite app to use that helps you organize your life? Of the list, which app will you be downloading?
