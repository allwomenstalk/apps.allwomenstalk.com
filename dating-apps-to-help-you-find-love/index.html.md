# 9 Dating Apps to Help You Find Love ...

Good news for all the ladies out there looking for love! Here are 9 amazing must-have apps that will not only introduce you to the dating world but also significantly increase your chances of finding your perfect match! ❤️ 💕

## 1. Tinder

## 2. Happn

_ Happn is different from other dating apps since it lets you discover people you've crossed paths with in real life! When this happens, their profile will show up plus you're able to see the number of times you've crossed paths with someone as well as the time and place of your last encounter!

play.google.com_

## 3. OkCupid Dating

_ If you don't like all the hype going around about Tinder (maybe it's just too mainstream for you) but you like its style, OkCupid is one you simply must try! With this app you're able to browse and connect with matches nearby as well as let others you know you're interested in them!

## 4. Double

_ Double lets you sign up with a friend (or someone you know) as well as liking or disliking other pairs nearby! 'Double Trouble!' happens when a pair likes you back, which then allows you to have a fun group chat all within the app, plus the chance to arrange a double date!

play.google.com_

## 5. RSVP

I think it's pretty clear why RSVP is so popular in Australia! Just a few of its many features include the ability to live chat, save your latest searches and send romantic 'kisses' which is a great way to say hey to singles that catch your eye!

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/au/app/rsvp-australias-no-1-dating/id397280080?ign-mpt=uo%3D2)

## 6. Bumble

_ Ladies, now you're totally in charge and get to take the lead when it comes to dating via Bumble! This is because with Bumble ladies always talk first. But there's a catch: you only have 24 hours to make the first move or else the connection expires! Better be quick to seal the deal!

play.google.com_

_ Seeing potential matches around you has never been easier than on POF, where a whole list of users currently nearby is shown for you to see a single glance who catches your eye! Then message and get to know all your favourite matches as much as you like! .
