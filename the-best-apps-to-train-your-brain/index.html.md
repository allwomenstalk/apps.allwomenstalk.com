# The Best Apps to Train Your Brain  ...

The scientific community is still evaluating the benefits of brain training. It is generally agreed that there are benefits and that brain training can help with improving cognitive skills and memory. I am of the opinion that simply, your brain needs to be exercised like any other part of your body. If you aren’t using and challenging your brain it can become lazy. So whether you want to get a better memory or just want to stay sharp, here are some apps to try:

## 1. Fit Brains Trainer

_ There are over 360 puzzles and games on this app you can try in order to train your brain to think a little more quickly. There are plenty of gaming levels where you can increase the level of difficulty to your liking.
itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_

## 2. Memory Trainer

_ This app takes you through a series of exercises that help you remember things from phone numbers to tasks you need to complete. The exercises help you recall information more easily.
play.google.com_

## 3. Happify

_ With this app, you may push the unhappy thoughts out of your head by replacing them with happy thoughts. There are quizzes, polls and a controversial gratitude journal.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/happify/id730601963?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
play.google.com_

## 4. Eidetic

![Eidetic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/go/fwd0cuzk564c72ef38200340904330_288x411.jpg)

This app helps you train your brain with spaced repetition. With it, you will learn a technique that allows you to quickly memorize phone numbers, names and your friend’s birthdays.
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.yourelink.Eidetic&amp;hl=en)
[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/dJqq2L)

## 5. Lumosity

![Lumosity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/sc/om3ui2d5564c72eef1715880494839_288x512.jpg)

If you want to improve your memory, flexibility of thinking, processing speed, attention or problem solving skills, then try the games on the Lumosity App.
[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lumoslabs.lumosity&amp;hl=en_US)
[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/JNgjXd)

## 6. Personal Zen

![Personal Zen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zm/ka/w3waag24564c72ef506b6977503247_320x560.jpg)

This app will help you maintain a calm state of mind. It will help you relax so that your brain may get a little respite before you challenge it with something new and fresh.
[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/personal-zen/id689013447?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

## 7. Elevate

![Elevate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/od/kqi7i8bq564c72ef65e13011495889_322x523.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/elevate-brain-training/id875063456?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

## 8. Braingle

_ Improve your reasoning skills with this app by working through a series of optical illusions and riddles. There are over 15,000 brainteasers and the app is free too.
itunes.apple.com_

## 9. 150+ Brain Teasers

## 10. Mind Games

![Mind Games](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/hr/ft2q53x7564c72f102bee418768573_322x572.jpg)

## 12. Brain It on

![Brain It on](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/w4/u0bcqj1p564c72f11b7b3027698541_600x338.jpg)

## 13. Brain Trainer Special

_ This is a collection of games you can use to improve your thinking skills with a few simple brain-training exercises.
play.google.com_

## 14. Cognifit Brain Fitness

![Cognifit Brain Fitness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/zw/nffumiwb564c72f26b3be991201688_322x551.jpg)

## 15. BrainHQ
