# 17 Super Cute Apps to Download ASAP ...

With so many apps on our phones dedicated to the boring tasks of adulting, we could all use a little cuteness to combat the mobile banking and bill-paying, don't you agree? Here are a few super-cute apps to add a little fun and "aww" to your day.

## 1. Neko Atsume

![Neko Atsume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/wo/w84j7ai856d8a1c8f0d84020301284_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/neko-atsume-kitty-collector/id923917775)
You'll find this kitty-collecting app in the games section, but it's really not a game at all. You just set out virtual treats and watch the make-believe kittens come to your yard. Cuteness overload!

## 2. Tiny Wings

![Tiny Wings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/b2/sox1fwdf56d8a1c901f90567492549_520x293.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tiny-wings/id417817520)
Another addictive game, this one with sweet little birds (and are those frogs?). The music is just as darling as the little bird themselves... it's so calming!

## 3. Hatch

![Hatch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qx/c1/u807x9b956d8a1c92ed5c671682167_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hatch/id718835727)
If you're a 90s kid, you remember the Tamagotchi, little egg-shaped electronic pets you took care of (or didn't take care of, and then had to deal with the guilt for weeks). This app features a little critter you need to care for, too, and if there's ever been a more precious virtual pet than him, I haven't seen it.

## 4. Monsters Ate My Birthday Cake

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
It sounds like a reasonable premise for a game, right? And this isn't just any game. It's the cleverest, cutest game ever.

## 5. Trees of Doom

![Trees of Doom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/wq/udr81at856d8a1c99fce7653511413_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ninjatown-trees-of-doom/id369997638)
This is a game, and an addictive one at that! Your goal is to get your adorable little ninja up into the trees. It's harder, and cuter, than it sounds!

## 6. Lost Yeti

![Lost Yeti](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/iv/q187fjtj56d8a1ca91f49204007194_520x293.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/E4Hml2)
The yeti is cute, and the game environments are cute, both in a very retro 1980s arcade sort of way. I keep waiting for Link to appear.

## 7. Oquonie

![Oquonie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s3/to/pa1g5shs56d8a1cb72378975337965_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/oquonie/id819777958?ign-mpt=uo%3D8)
I don't know if "cute" is the best word to describe this game, but the drawings are striking in a lot of ways... some of them are cute, others are just really cool.

## 8. Alphabear

![Alphabear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/le/gnumpwvv56d8a1cc80533895736684_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/alphabear-word-puzzle-game/id930003798)
It's as cute as it is educational, a word game with sweet squarish bears to make it fun.

## 9. Tiny Tower

![Tiny Tower](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/am/ovy1l3ls56d8a1cdda382850626093_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tiny-tower-free-city-building/id422667065)
This tower-building game is old-school bit-tastic! The characters who inhabit your tower are really cute and funny, and there are so many of them!

## 10. Nyawatobi

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
A game about jump-roping? That can't possibly be fun, interesting, or cute... can it? Yes. Yes it can.

## 11. Disney Tsum Tsum

![Disney Tsum Tsum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/fy/vwm72mrh56d8a1d0860f0248298611_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/line-disney-tsum-tsum/id867964741)
If you love the squishy little real-life tsum tsum stuffies, you'll really like this match-3 style game, too.

## 12. Patchmania

![Patchmania](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/r3/yqxqyuuj56d8a1d17feac227153639_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/patchmania-puzzle-about-bunny/id807323207)
Don't let the cute exterior of this bunny fool you - he's on a mission for revenge!

## 13. Pug Run

![Pug Run](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/db/u6/r4r3z5dr56d8a1d1d7612798533988_600x338.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pug-run/id588432226)
Bogart is a pug on a mission. Won't you help him along? I mean, of course the answer is yes... how could you resist his puppy cuteness?

## 14. Hello Kitty Fun Park

![Hello Kitty Fun Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jo/i9/voq6vwez56d8a1d21912f119452696_600x338.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/JtOzwC)
Hello Kitty's been the cutest cartoon cat (or is she a girl?) for decades, so it's no wonder her latest game made the list.

## 15. Bumpy Road

![Bumpy Road](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/me/f1wzbj9y56d8a1d224862808308141_568x320.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bumpy-road/id423855879)
While the "cute" in most of these apps has to do with a wee sweet animal, this game features an old couple even more adorable than Carl and Ellie Fredrickson. No really!

## 16. Cut the Rope 2

![Cut the Rope 2](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/ob/rl9gx31556d8a1d23cb29897629191_500x572.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/YjUBgc)
Om Nom's super-cute adventures continue with new friends and new worlds. This game is just as addictive as the first one, so be prepared!
