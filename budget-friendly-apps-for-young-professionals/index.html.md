# 7 Budget\-friendly Apps for Young Professionals ...

Whether you're a young professional trying to make ends meet, or just someone who's looking to save money (aren't we all?), you're going to love these budget-friendly apps. You might even be kicking yourself when you realize you haven't been using these apps all the while! What could be easier than having a money-saving tip right on your smartphone? Check out these budget-friendly apps and keep your wallet happy.

## 1. ShopSavvy

![ShopSavvy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/os/a68l1knl_320x480.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/jYKHHS)

One of the many budget-friendly apps you're going to want to give a try is ShopSavvy. The bar code scanner feature will allow you to scan a product and search for the same product in other stores to compare prices. You can also "like" products to get price alerts and create and share lists with friends to see what others are buying.

## 2. Gas Buddy

![Gas Buddy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/m2/upsl4kif_506x900.jpg)

**On Google Play at:** [play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=gbis.gbandroid&amp;hl=en)

Ever fill your tank up with gas, then drive down the street just to find another station with a much better price? You won't have to experience that again with Gas Buddy. With this app, you can find the cheapest gas nearby, and report the price of gas at any station to help the fellow app users.

## 3. RetailMeNot

![RetailMeNot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/km/ilncuipa_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/retailmenot-coupons/id521207075?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

Avoid printing and clipping coupons with this wonderful virtual coupon app. Choose your favorite stores from the list provided (there's just about every major store you could think of) and stay updated daily on specials and new coupons, both in store and online.

## 4. Hotel Tonight

![Hotel Tonight](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/ds/msnvzfhj_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hotel-tonight/id407690035?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

If you're comfortable being a bit spontaneous and not planning your hotel accommodations far in advance, Hotel Tonight is just about the best travel deal you can get. Hotels will send their rooms that haven't been booked over to Hotel Tonight, who then gives you an unbeatable deal for that day. You can stay in luxury for a fraction of the price, all by waiting 'til the last minute. Sometimes procrastination can pay off!

## 5. Scoutmob

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

To get the best deals on restaurants, bars, and cafes, you're gonna want to download the Scoutmob app. You'll also get up-to-date info on events happening in your town! The only downside? Scoutmob is only available in a select number of major cities, so your local town may not be featured.

## 6. ATM Hunter

![ATM Hunter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sw/kr/k3z2vb62_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/JzrY09)

Ever been in a pinch for cash but don't want to pay that annoying ATM fee? ATM Hunter will find your bank's ATM so you can avoid the fee and cash-out quick. Cha-ching!

## 7. Expensify

![Expensify](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/ul/o15j91dv_506x900.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/expensify-expense-reports/id471713959?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

Expensify is your pocket-accountant. Link your credit cards and bank accounts to the app so you can track all of your spending, and even upload pictures of your receipts to cover all grounds. Managing your money really has never been easier!

Between groceries, rent, and leisure, it may seem pretty exhausting having to spend a great deal of money every month. These apps help take the load off a bit by giving you great deals and helping you to manage your funds a bit better. They're even a little fun to use too! We like that these apps take the pain and nuisance out of budgeting and watching what you spend. Do you have a favorite money-saving app? Please share in the comments!
