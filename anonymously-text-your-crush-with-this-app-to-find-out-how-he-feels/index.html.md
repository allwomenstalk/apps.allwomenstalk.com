# Anonymously Text Your Crush with This App to Find out How He Feels ...

_ If you want to know how your crush feels about you, but are too nervous to ask him the question yourself, you can use an app to help you out. According to [Teen Vogue,](https://www.teenvogue.com/story/kiss-app-sam-lurye-crushes?mbid=social_twitter) Kiss_ will let you send your crush a lineup of several names (so that he doesn't know you're the one actually interested) and asks him to admit whether or not he feels the same way about you. That way, if you find out he's interested, you can ask him out without the fear of rejection!
