# 7 Great Apps for Tv Lovers ...

There are some truly amazing apps for TV lovers. If you're anything like me, you probably spend a good amount of your day on one of these apps (whoops). Hey, who can blame us? It's summer time and the summer is definitely for binge-watching TV shows! Check out some of these awesome apps for TV lovers and then let me know which ones YOU can't live without.

## 1. Netflix

![Netflix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/at/jnwrmefy_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/netflix/id363590051?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free (with subscription)

Must I really explain why this app tops the list? It's the world's leading subscription service for watching TV episodes and movies on your phone. This mobile app delivers the best experience anywhere, anytime! With your Netflix membership (which is SO worth getting), you can use this free app and instantly watch your favorite TV shows and discover new obsessions. I spend way too much time on this app but I'm not even sorry about it! This is just one of many great apps for TV lovers.

## 2. Imdb

![Imdb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/yf/pqkcifln_322x551.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/imdb-movies-tv/id342792525?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free

Do you ever watch a show, recognize a familiar face, but totally blank on where you know that actor from?! That's where this app comes in handy. You can easily look up the actor's name and find out all the project that (s)he has been involved in. IMDb is the world's largest collection of movie, TV, and celebrity info! On the app, you can find trailers, photos, reviews, quotes, and so much more about any show.

## 3. Hulu plus

![Hulu plus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r0/fh/tt7beipn_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hulu-plus/id376510438?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free (with subscription)

With a Hulu Plus paid subscription, you can use this free app to stream all your favorite hit TV shows. You can watch the full current season of popular shows like Modern Family, Family Guy, and many others. You can also enjoy classics like Lost (which is totally binge-worthy). If you're a TV lover, you can't go wrong with this app.

## 4. Tv Guide

![Tv Guide](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/x2/ejrh0jmk_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/LxIl2W)
**Price:** Free

TV Guide's mobile app is your one-stop 24/7 TV companion! It makes TV simple again. You can organize your favorite shows, sport teams, and actors to find where you can watch them. You can see what's trending (a social hot list based on what other users are watching) and check out the local TV listings.

## 5. Watch Abc

![Watch Abc](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/ug/n92521ya_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/6ftdxC)
**Price:** Free

There are so many TV channels that offer mobile apps. Watch ABC just so happens to be my favorite because it's home to some of TV's best shows. On the app, you can stream shows like Grey's Anatomy, Scandal, Nashville, and so much more. Are you binge-watching any of those shows? I can't wait for the new seasons!

## 6. Hbo Go

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free (with TV subscription)

If you can't get enough of HBO's hit shows like Game of Thrones, you NEED this app. You can stream all the seasons of your current favorite shows. You can even stream classics like my favorite show, Entourage.

## 7. Fios Mobile

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free

This app is awesome. It offers free and subscription On Demand content, plus the ability to use your device as a remote control (talk about convenient, huh?!) and access the entire FiOS On Demand catalog. I'll admit, I use this app way too often. Whenever I can't find my remote, FiOS mobile is always there to rescue me!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many fantastic apps to download if you're a TV lover! Of the list, which app is your favorite?
