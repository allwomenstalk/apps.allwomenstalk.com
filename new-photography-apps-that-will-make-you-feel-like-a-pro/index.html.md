# 7 New Photography Apps That Will Make You Feel like a Pro ...

There are so many awesome new photography apps that are worth downloading! If you've always got your phone out to capture everything around you, these apps will eventually come in handy. Wow all of your friends with your sweet photos using these new photography apps!

## 1. Vsco Cam

![Vsco Cam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/ko/hjtq2z22_322x469.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/vsco-cam/id588013838)
**Price:** Free
VSCO Cam is the best way to shoot, edit, and share all of your photographs. You can find and follow other creative artists around the world and post/share your photos with them! What's AWESOME about this app? The number of followers, comments, and like are totally absent from the platform. That's because beautiful imagery trumps your "social rank." You can publish all your original content to your Journal to inspire others, while exploring others to find your own inspiration. This is just one of many new photography apps that'll have you feeling like a pro.

## 2. Horizon

![Horizon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/yx/vo0y9xok_322x572.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/horizon-horizontal-video-photo/id778576249)
**Price:** $1.99
With Horizon, you can record horizontal photos no matter how you hold your phone! That means you can hold your phone upright and still be capturing your moment as if you were holding your phone sideways. This app definitely comes in handy for me! Whenever I'm at a concert, this app is everything.

## 3. Frontback

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
This app can only be described in one word... AMAZING. With this app, you can capture and share what you see and how you feel at any moment. The app allows you take a photo with front and back camera and share it in one single image! I can't even begin to tell you how long I've wanted this to be possible. You can post the photo in real time and then explore other feeds from around the world. You can also interact with the app's community by leaving a photo or 5 second video reaction to their Frontback photo!

## 4. Project Life

![Project Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/l7/xb6j5e6s_322x572.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/project-life/id915861546)
**Price:** $2.99
With the Project Life app, you can scrapbook your life without having to actually scrapbook it. It takes simplicity to a whole new level! The app features built-in templates and pre-designed artwork that help your virtual scrapbook pages come together in no time. You can import photos from your Camera Roll, Photo Stream, or Dropbox. You can create your entire scrapbook in the palm of your hand and then... are you ready for this? You can get those pages printed and made into actual, real scrapbooks! You'll have everything you want without having to put in any work.

## 5. Looksee

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
Wanna collaborate with top photographers? Looksee makes it possible! In the Filter Pack Marketplace, you can apply unique photographic style to your photos and then share them instantly on Facebook, Twitter, and any other social media account!

## 6. Eye'em

![Eye'em](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sy/ee/oua1qsue_322x572.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/eyeem-photo-filter-camera/id445638931)
**Price:** Free
With EyeEm, you can shoot, edit and get creative with your photography. The app features unique filters and effects that allow you to connect with new talents! You can also discover and follow new album based on your interests!

## 7. Pomelo

![Pomelo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/gv/q0d3jqjm_322x572.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/d9b8mN)
**Price:** Free
On the app, you can choose between a huge collection of filters that are beloved by fellow photographers. You can freely adjust the strength of the filters and use them in real-time to capture whatever you're seeing!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome new photography apps that will have you feeling like a professional! Have you downloaded any of these? Which photography apps can't you get enough of?
