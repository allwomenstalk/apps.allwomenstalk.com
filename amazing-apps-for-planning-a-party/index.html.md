# 7 Amazing Apps for Planning a Party ...

There are so many fantastic and convenient apps for planning a party. You no longer have to stress over writing down your to-do list, guest-list, etc. because you can store it all on your phone now. If you plan parties/events regularly, I definitely recommend looking into these apps. It'll make your life so much easier! Check out some of the cool apps for planning a party and let me know which ones work best for you.

## 1. Speakeasy Cocktails

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $9.99
Even though this app is a bit on the expensive side, it's well worth the splurge if you're looking to make some awesome cocktails for your guests. The app offers more than 40 instructional videos (featuring award-winning mixologists) and is packed with information and interactive recipes. You'll learn the different techniques and discover all the supplies that you need to accomplish it all. You'll definitely impress your friends with this! "Speakeasy Cocktails" is just one of many awesome apps for planning a party.

## 2. Hello Vino

![Hello Vino](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/gi/puv0sgcl_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/2okfsJ)
**Price:** Free
This app is pretty incredible. There are so many different wine options that it's sure to confuse even the best of us. This app will help you whether you're trying to decide what wine to gift your host or serve your guests. All you have to do is tell the app what you're eating and it'll give you a pairing suggestion. You can even save all the wines you liked/disliked so you can remember that for your next soiree.

## 3. Spotify

![Spotify](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/pv/rsf7a65r_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/spotify-music/id324684580?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
What's a party without music? Spotify allows you to easily become the party DJ and find a playlist for any moment. The app is free for the first 48 hours so keep that in mind before downloading it. Of course, after those two days, you can upgrade to Spotify Premium ($10 a month) and be able to stream anything in the Spotify library.

## 4. Simple Soiree

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $1.99
This app was created by an actual event planner so that means it's full of tried-and-tested tips. It'll help you plan your party, menu, guest-list, and then help you coordinate with all of your guests.

## 5. Heads up!

![Heads up!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/g5/b8vtompj_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/heads-up/id623592465?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $0.99
No party is complete without this charades-meets-catch-phrase game! It was developed by Ellen DeGeneres and will have you and your friends on the floor laughing. There are so many fun categories to choose from like "Pop Star Overload" where your pop-culture knowledge is tested and "Act It Out" where you make a fool out of yourself LOL! The app is only $0.99 and in-app purchases are available if you want more categories.

## 6. Event Planning

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** $0.99
Not only does this app help you keep all your party worries organized, it offers you helpful hints too. I think the app is well-worth the $0.99 if you ask me!

## 7. Party & Event Planner

![Party & Event Planner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/iz/dyftruxw_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/party-event-planner-lite/id500792894?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
With this app, you can organize an event in a quick and efficient way. When you tell the app the date of your party, it'll compute your party countdown and help you to accomplish all the necessary tasks in a timely fashion. The task list ensures that nothing will be overlooked, ensuring that you'll have a fantastic time at your party.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome apps to download when planning a party/event. These apps are sure to diminish the headaches and help you have an easier time! Of the list, which app do you find the most useful?
