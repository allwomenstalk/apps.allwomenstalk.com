# Girl's Guide to Make Your Online Dating Profile Photo Stand out ...

Want to know **how to make your online dating profile photo stand out**? With the world of online dating websites and apps being so popular in this modern age of romance, it can be almost impossible to make sure that you stand out from the crowd! It might not be completely ideal, but there is no denying that the main factor in a person’s success when dating online is their [profile photo](https://www.allure.com/story/how-to-take-good-selfies). You would like to think that a really great match will be able to see beneath the surface and get to love the person behind the picture, but in order for initial contact to made, you can’t get around the fact that your photo needs to do the business! Here are some tips on **how to make your online dating profile photo stand out**.

## 1. Multiple Pics

Give the people something to scroll through by uploading _two to four pictures_. Don’t just leave them with a single one to make their mind up on. Provide a couple of different situations and different angles so that they can start to get a better sense of who and what you are as a person, rather than just an on-screen avatar. This is one of the best answers for **how to make your online dating profile photo stand out**.

## 2. Be Real

It’s only fair that you be realistic about your photo choices! There is no point in using a pic from ten years ago because you are going to be found out as soon as you meet up with someone! The more real you are online, the more successful you will be when it comes to the face to face interactions that follow.

## 3. Not Just Selfies

Selfies are a good way to show off your beautiful face, but ideally, you should be giving people more than that to go on. Include a couple of [portraits and full body images](https://expertphotography.com/21-creative-self-portrait-photography-ideas/) as well just so potential romantic interests can get the entire picture. You really can’t tell much from a selfie, and they can be so edited these days.

## 4. Camera Confidence

People who look happy and confident in their pictures have a much higher success rate than those who look shy and bashful. If you look straight into the camera, it will make people feel like you are looking right at them, and that is a great place to start in terms of building a connection. And don’t forget to smile!

## 5. Bright Colours

Make the most of modern phone and computer screens by choosing pics where you are wearing bright, vibrant colours. These colours will help to [catch people's attention](https://www.instyle.co.uk/how-tos/how-to-find-best-color-to-wear-for-your-skin-tone) and make you stand out from the crowd.

## 6. Pets

If you have a pet, then include at least [one photo](https://manypets.com/uk/articles/how-to-take-good-selfies-with-your-pets/) with them in it! There are plenty of animal lovers out there who see connecting with a fellow animal lover as their number one dating priority. So it’s good to let them know that you are a fellow pet owner right off the bat!
