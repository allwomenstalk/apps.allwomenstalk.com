# 4 Genius Ways to Attract More Readers to Your Blog  ...

Are you looking for **ways to attract more readers to your blog**? It doesn’t matter whether you are a new or seasoned blogger, you will always need to know how to attract new readers and keep them interested. There are some useful **ways to attract more readers to your blog**. Here they are:

## 1. Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization is just one of the **ways to attract more readers to your blog**. With this you are giving permission to the Internet (Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc.) to discover you and make you accessible to a wide audience.

## 2. Google Analytics

Using and adding Google Analytics to your blog can help you get more information about your site traffic. Analytics has information about how much time people spend on your website, how often they visit, whether they leave immediately or not, the average time people spend on the blog, etc. Google Analytics breaks down your traffic so you can see where changes need to be made.

## 3. Social Networking

Use Facebook, Twitter, Google+, or anything else, you can to promote your blog. Engaging with your readers brings real time information and feedback.

## 4. Online Communities

Networking with readers that have similar interests means that you can gain loyal followers with greater ease. Participate in forums and online communities to meet people with similar interests to yours. You're sure to see your blog traffic increasing very quickly.
