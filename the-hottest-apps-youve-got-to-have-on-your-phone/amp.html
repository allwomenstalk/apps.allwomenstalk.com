The Hottest Apps You've Got to Have on Your Phone ...

Instagram • DriveSafe Mode • Moodnotes • Spruce • Hopper • More ...

By Neecey

So many apps, so little time. There’s an app for everything – and more. I’ve pulled together a little list of some random apps that are hot property and will definitely help you out in all areas of your life.

1. Instagram

Share your moments with the world and see those moments everyone else has shared. Fabulous inspiration and plenty of practical tips too.

Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

2. DriveSafe Mode

I think all drivers should have this app. It trains you to keep your eyes on the road and to resist the temptation to pick up your phone when driving.
Cost: Free
drivesafemode.com

3. Moodnotes

Want to keep track of your emotions but don’t want to keep a journal or diary? You’ll love moodnotes. Gives you a fun, techy way to get a handle on your emotional journey.
Cost: $3.99
itunes.apple.com

4. Spruce

I’m not sure how I feel about apps that connect you to a “medical diagnosis” but the option is there. This one is for all things dermatological. Upload a pic, enter the symptoms and a certified dermatologist will give a diagnosis.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

5. Hopper

Even if you only fly once a year, you need this app. It’s all you need in one handy place – track ticket prices and book great deals.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

6. Period Tracker

Every woman can do with some help with understanding their menstrual cycle. There are loads of apps to make it easy – this is the simplest and most user-friendly.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

7. Switch

You never know when you might need look for a new job. There are lots of job hunting apps but you might like this one because it’s a “swipe” app.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

8. Medium

Looking for a new news and story provider? Try this one!
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

9. Mastery

Apparently, to become a true master of something takes 10,000 hours. This app helps you track your progress and more.
Cost: Free
play.google.com

10. Snapmoji

Do you like to punctuation your communications with emojis and gifs and love taking selfies? You will fall in love with this entertaining app.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com

11. Colorfy

If you are among those enjoying the growing trend in adult coloring you must get this app. It’s perfect for when you can’t bust out the book and pencils.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

12. Penny

This is a genuine contender to Mint. It’s far more fun and personal. Yes managing your finances can be fun!
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

13. Committo3

Track your progress towards your goals and monitor your resolutions by making three commitments per day.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

14. Lingvist

Learn a new language anywhere you like – in 200 hours.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

15. Allrecipes

If you’re going to use an app to make meals, at least make it easy and use an app that has video tutorials. Like this one.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

16. Square Cash

This is ideal if you have a group of friends with whom you regularly go out and have to split bills.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

17. Uber

Every girl should have Uber on her phone but if you're not happy with Uber, try Lyft instead.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

18. Evernote

It's been around for like, ever, but it remains the most recommended app for note taking (and more)
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

19. Snapchat

The must have accessory for your social life. Message your friends and share photos, videos, text, and drawings.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

20. 1Password

Do you struggle with remembering all your passwords? With 1Password, you only have to remember - yep you guessed it - one. And it's a secure wallet too.
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

21. Magisto

Make your smartphone videos look like they were produced by a pro, and it takes just a few seconds
Cost: Free
itunes.apple.com
play.google.com

What app can’t you live without?
