# The Hottest Love Apps You Need in Your Phone ...

Apps can help out in all areas of your love life. Need help finding a date? Want to stay in touch? Want a special place to share things that have meaning to your both? Want to spice up bedroom activity? There’s an app for that, and more … many more.

## 1. Tinder

![mobile phone, product, gadget, advertising, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/so/b55fby5r586ff8b55df01575557612_500x696.jpg)

When it comes to finding love on a smartphone, Tinder is still very much king of the castle! The picture swiping app is more popular right now that it has ever been, with people starting to move away from a casual hook up type of thing into looking for a much longer lasting, meaningful connection. Basically, if you are single and you don’t have Tinder, then get straight to it!

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tinder/id547702041)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tinder&hl=en_US)

## 2. Avocado

_ If you have already bagged a partner and don’t need to do the Tinder swiping, then why not download Avocado, a private messaging app that lets you share personal and intimate chats with your loved one. It’s filled with lots of fun customisable elements that makes your conversations much more exciting than just texting or Whatsapp.

itunes.apple.com_

_play.google.com_

## 3. LoveByte

_ LoveByte is a cool relationship app that you can use to organise everything to do with you and your loved one all in one easy place. When this app is set up, you will never do things like miss an anniversary or forget to send a goodnight text when you are apart, it will always be there to remind of you of the big and little things!

itunes.apple.com_

_play.google.com_

## 4. Kouply

_ Kouply is a really fun app that encourages couples to be more connected to one another through small things like interactive games, personalised activity suggestions and a completely private timeline where you can share and store memories of your relationship.

itunes.apple.com_

## 5. Kindu

![color, text, font, product, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xy/vg/p6q8d6ik586ff8b6837b8884199054_500x696.jpg)

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/kindu-intimacy-ideas-for-couples/id346524753)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=us.kindu.kinduforcouples&hl=en_us)

Kindu is an app that helps you to rediscover the joys of intimacy with your partner if you are going through a bit of a rough patch. With lots of fun suggestions for partner activities and a safe messaging space where you can share you deepest desires, it’s bound to be exciting!

## 6. Fix a Fight

_ Fix A Fight is a quirky and innovative way to resolve an argument with your partner. The fun app allows you to get your feelings about the row out in the open and it then encourages you to make amends and get back to loving one another!

itunes.apple.com_

## 7. 69 Places

![Recycling, text, font, screenshot, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m9/v9/xf0lob0k586ff8b885d80771811216_500x551.jpg)

And once you have done all that making up, you can get adventurous with your making out by downloading this cheekily named app that suggests 69 different locations where you should get down and dirty!

[itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/mFRcK7)

## 8. Wickr

![text, font, line, brand, shape,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xo/b7/maxk0zfp586ff8b908262034590024_500x696.jpg)

If you want a super secure messaging app in order to send sexy pics and videos to your loved one, then Wickr is definitely the one for you. Your posts can last between 3 seconds and 6 days, and unlike Snapchat, the app guarantees that nothing is saved!

[itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wickr-me-private-secure-messenger/id528962154)

[play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mywickr.wickr2&hl=en_us)

## 9. Happy Couple

_ Want to know more about your partner? This is a fun app that tests what you know and what you don’t know about your SO. Earn points as you quiz and the app has handy tips to help your relationship grow stronger.
itunes.apple.com_
_play.google.com_

If there's an area of your love life that needs some attention or improvement, just search for a suitable app.
