# 7 Funny Women on Twitter You Should Be following ...

As someone who loves both Twitter and the thoughts of funny women, I’m always looking for funny women to follow on Twitter. Whether they became famous simply for their funny tweets, or found fame years ago for other reasons, these funny women are the people you should be following on Twitter.

## 1. Kelly Oxford

![Kelly Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/km/tvb7yvi054df7fad73036311552950_347x500.jpg)

Kelly Oxford is one of the few people on the Internet who have created an entire career by cultivating a hilarious Twitter account. Once a stay-at-home mom, Kelly Oxford took to Twitter after a history in blogging, and what resulted was a career in books and TV. She’s now a New York Times bestselling author, a screenwriter, and moved with her family to Los Angeles to pursue her career. Yes, that’s how funny she is.

## 2. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/zs/xdtvw20t54df801c4fc12188025915_600x900.jpg)

If you only know of Nicole Richie from her Simple Life days, you are sincerely missing out. While she’s gone on to be successful in a variety of different fields and careers, her Twitter feed is where she truly shines. If you’ve watched her new reality show, Candidly Nicole, you know that her Twitter feed is so funny that it’s somewhat integrated into the show!

## 3. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hf/qz/rkuy9p5g54df80461fbf2584345719_600x870.jpg)

You probably know Anna Kendrick from her role as Becca in Pitch Perfect, as well as a slew of other roles in both indie and Oscar-nominated films. If you haven’t already followed her on Twitter because you’re a fan of her acting work, you should simply because she’s one of the funniest people on Twitter.

## 4. Grace Helbig

![Grace Helbig](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d3/d7/elxp77rw54df80c07a61c881563125_600x900.jpg)

Although YouTube is a career that many people are vying for these days, a few years ago, Grace was one of few. As a result of her own insane work ethic, Grace now has a New York Times bestselling book, a TV show with E! on the way, and a hilarious Twitter account to boot. Follow her if you’re looking for wild and weird observations on her day-to-day life.

## 5. Ashley Perez

![Ashley Perez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/sq/yhm9im3r54df813045257418385716_600x800.jpg)

Unless you’re an avid fan of Buzzfeed, you probably have yet to hear of Ashley Perez. Ashley stars on one of Buzzfeed’s many YouTube channels, Buzzfeed Violet. On Buzzfeed Violet, Ashley makes hilarious and relatable videos about anything and everything, but mostly they’re about being a girl and her love for Taylor Swift, and that’s mostly what her tweets are about too.

## 6. Chrissy Teigen

![Chrissy Teigen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/uk/kqhq2aev54df821a397b4933663381_600x900.jpg)

You probably recognize Chrissy Teigen from the cover of your brother’s issue of the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Edition. You may also know her as John Legend’s wife, but what she should be known for are her hilarious tweets. She’s not afraid to call people out, specifically people who hate her, but she’s also so sarcastic that you may feel like you’ve found your comedic soul mate.

## 7. Keough Novak

![Keough Novak](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wx/sy/og5bfypy54df829b1aee0120184269_600x866.jpg)

You probably haven’t heard about Keough Novak just yet, but give it time, you will. She’s B.J. (of The Office) Novak’s (cough, cough, fictional) little sister. She’s recently worked with companies like Hello Giggles and MTV Style, so she’s definitely on the rise! She’s one of the sassiest teenagers on the Internet, which is saying a lot, but you’ll probably find yourself laughing along with a lot of her tweets!

Which funny women should I follow on Twitter? Do you follow any of these women on Twitter? Give me new names I need to check out in the comments!
