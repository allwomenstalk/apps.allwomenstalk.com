# The 5 Best Online Dating Sites ...

Wondering which ones are the **best online dating sites**? Dating isn’t quite like what it used to be. Long phone calls have been replaced by quick snap chats. Uber X now takes the place of limousines, and a night at the movies is now binge watching Grey's Anatomy on Netflix. It’s no surprise in this digital age that so many love stories are now occurring online.

In fact, did you know that Tinder has [7 million monthly users](https://www.bustle.com/articles/154205-heres-how-many-women-vs-men-use-tinder), half of which are men, so the odds are in your favor.

However, if you’d rather take a chance on an online dating site, here are some of the **best online dating sites**.

## 1. EHarmony

eHarmony is one of the original dating sites. If you’re looking for long-term relationships or marriage this is definitely one that you should try out since it tops the list of **best online dating sites.**

It’s been around for over 15 years and has led to [4% of marriages](https://mashable.com/roundup/best-dating-sites?test_uuid=07HgWq9fWw2Ea4XeFZENW2X&test_variant=a) over the years. Right now eHarmony has people from 150 countries.

There is a monthly subscription, so be sure to find some coupon codes before you sign up.

## 2. Match

Match is another extremely popular online dating site. Around [24% of online daters use this site](https://onlineforlove.com/eharmony-vs-match/) which is one of the highest percentages in the market.

It’s been around even longer than eHarmony, and its simple and fun features will ensure that it’s around for a long time to come as well.

It’s a great site for everyone, from your grandmother to your cousin.

## 3. OkCupid

OkCupid focuses on finding people that have the same ideals as you. Before searching for matches you’ll answer a large variety of questions. Some are serious and politically motivated, while others are more casual.

This helps you to find real meaningful relationships formed on commonly held beliefs.

## 4. Zoosk

Zoosk is another great dating app for someone looking for an international fling. This site is designed with millennials in mind. It keeps track of your activity and sends you people that are compatible with your actions.

It’s easy to use and has been described as a fun, flirty dating site.

## 5. Plenty of Fish

While many of these sites have a monthly subscription, Plenty of Fish does not. This is a great site if you just want to explore the waters of Internet dating without having to make a monthly investment.

Even with the best online dating site, you won’t have much success if you don’t put time into your online dating profile. Snap a couple of selfies, find the best filter, and get started finding your next love.
