# 7 Must\-Have Photography Apps for Beginners ...

If you've been looking for some must have photography apps for beginners, you're not alone. I too have been searching for some apps that will help improve the quality of my pictures. A good app can turn your ordinary photos into extraordinary images. During my search I have found several great photography apps that I want to share with you. Here are 7 must have photography apps for beginners.

## 1. Instagram

![Instagram](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/g8/s1/g1k95nes_320x568.jpg)

**For iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/instagram/id389801252?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&amp%3Bign-mpt=uo%3D2)
**For Android:** [play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.instagram.android&amp;hl=en)

If you are looking for easy to use must have photography apps for beginners, Instagram is a great choice. It is also a free app, so you won't have to worry about paying for something that you're not going to use. You will be able to easily share the pictures you take with your friends and family. Instagram has unlimited storage, so feel free to snap as many photos as you like. You will also be able to easily shoot and share videos via Facebook and Twitter.

## 2. Facetune

![Facetune](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/uq/rd/r82wlrlj_320x568.jpg)

**For iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/facetune/id606310581?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

In my opinion, Facetune is one of the funnest editing apps available. If you've ever wanted to change your appearance, give this app a try. With this app, you can easily edit things like blemishes and laugh lines. You can use it to make your teeth appear whiter and to hide dark circles under your eyes. You can even use Facetune to change the shape of your eyes or lips.

## 3. Amateur Photography Magazine

![Amateur Photography Magazine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/g0/cq/na3unc83_320x568.jpg)

**For iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/amateur-photographer-magazine/id542233123?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

If you like having magazines delivered right to your phone, you should check out Amateur Photography Magazine. You can purchase weekly, monthly, or annual subscriptions. With this app, you will be able to see lots of tips and tricks for taking photos. You can also see camera and equipment reviews. Plus, you can view photo galleries that have thousands of great photos.

## 4. Photo Academy

_ **For iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**For Android:** [play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aspyresolutions.photoacademy)

If you are interested in becoming a professional photographer, but don't know where to start, you should try Photo Academy. It is packed full of great tips for new photographers. With this app you can see lots of tricks and techniques to use when taking photos. You will learn about shutter speeds and exposure modes. This app even tells you how to hold your camera for better picture quality.

## 5. Camera Awesome

_ **For iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**For Android:** _play.google.com_

This is one app that seems to have it all. It's also very user friendly. My favorite thing about Camera Awesome is that it has a low light boost, which is great for taking pictures in dark rooms. Editing your images with this app is quick. You can also share your pictures with friends and family on Facebook, Twitter, Flicker, and Smug Mug.

## 6. Pro HDR

_ **For iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**For Android:** _play.google.com_

If you're looking for a better quality HDR options, this is a good choice. With this app, you will easily be able to blend multiple images into one. Pro HDR offers both manual and automatic HDR options. This app allows you to easily adjust the brightness, contrast, warmth, saturation, and tint of your photos. Once you're done editing you can save your images or email it to a friend.

## 7. Noir Photo

![Noir Photo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/l9/ln/pk9rudbz_333x455.jpg)

**For iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/qoLvfa)

If you like fun apps, this is one you should try. This app offers full screen high resolution editing. It also gives you the option to create black and white or vignette images. You can quickly change the shape and size of each of the colors that are used on your images. Even though this app has a lot of features is surprisingly easy to use.

Taking and editing pictures can be a lot of fun if you've got the right equipment and software. I would like to know what you think. What great photography apps do you use?
