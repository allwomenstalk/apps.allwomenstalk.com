# The Bra Thatll Give You a Better Workout ...

According to [Elle,](https://www.elle.com/beauty/health-fitness/news/a33646/smart-sports-bra-fitness-tracker/) _My Zone_ has created an innovative new bra. What does it do that's so special? Well, it tracks your heart rate!

The bra even connects to an app, which will give you points whenever you exercise. It doesn't matter if you're running, rowing, or cycling, because you're going to get rewarded! Now doesn't that sound like fun? It'll push you to work out more than you usually do!

In the age of technology, there's no reason why our clothes shouldn't be connected to phone apps. Would you try wearing a bra like this and using the connecting app?
