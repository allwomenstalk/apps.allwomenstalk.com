# Dating App Hacks All Girls Who Swipe Should Know ...

Can you believe there was ever a time dating apps didn't exist? Yeah, us either, but it's true. Thanks to refinery29.com we've got all the scoop on Tinder hacks to help you find your next hunk. Check them out and see if any of them lead you to Mr. Right (or at least Mr. Right Now)...

## 1. Crowdsource Your Profile Photos

![feature phone, gadget, product, smartphone, mobile phone,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dh/eh/uty9ixms59197d16a495b267857827_600x399.jpg)

Need help deciding which photos deserve a spot on your profile? Get opinions from friends using Tinder's iMessage app, [Tinder Stacks](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/introducing-tinder-stacks-for-imessage-swipe-on-everything/). Within iMessage, you can create a "stack" of photos from your camera roll. Text this "stack" to friends, and they'll be able to easily swipe left or right on the pictures they like. Then, you can use their thoughts to make an informed decision.

## 2. Swipe on the Big Screen

![text, product, diagram, advertising, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ln/og/bqgrjbfj59197d1701993077204646_600x399.jpg)

Tinder wants to make swiping a family activity - or, at least, something to do with a group. Now, you can [swipe on Apple TV](https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2016/12/132933/tinder-apple-tv) with the Apple TV remote. When you match with someone, you continue the conversation via your phone, so that part stays private. But now, friends can weigh in without having to huddle around your phone.

## 3. Have More Options

![text, product, font, gadget, label,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/io/izszzx6w59197d173066f244120575_600x399.jpg)

Tinder has finally become more inclusive of [every gender identity](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/genders/). Now, when you go to your profile settings, you can choose "More" and type in the identity that best describes you.

## 4. Be a Matchmaker

![human action, text, brand, advertising, presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h0/qw/f1rperby59197d17705b5145853368_427x640.jpg)

If you see a share icon while swiping on Tinder, consider yourself one of the chosen ones: The app is [testing out](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/play-matchmaker-on-tinder/) a matchmaker feature with some of its users. If the tool is added for good, you'll be able to send a link of someone's profile to a friend, who can open the link and swipe left or right.

## 5. Figure out What You Want

![clothing, dress, gown, bridal clothing, bride,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jp/vu/fo18vxad59197d1825518887523687_600x1068.jpg)

Whether you're on a dating app in search of a serious relationship or just a casual fling, be transparent about what you want. In your bio, make sure whatever you write is reflective of what you're looking for, so you get matches who want the same.

## 6. Let the App Pick Photos for You

![text, product, gadget, brand, multimedia,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b3/y2/aome9dez59197d17d76ec543810313_600x515.jpg)

If you've ever had trouble figuring out which photo to put first, let Tinder decide. First, go to your profile settings and toggle on [Smart Photos](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/introducing-smart-photos-for-the-most-swipeworthy-you/). From there, the app automatically rotates the order of your photos and uses an algorithm to determine which order gets you the most right swipes.

## 7. Add a Filter

_ During campaign season, Bumble launched political filters. Now, post-election, there are drink filters_. Just go to your profile, and swipe right to see the options available. There are ones for smoothies, wine, whiskey, sparkling water, and coffee, so you're sure to find something that fits your personality.

## 8. Expand Your Dating Pool

_ If you're planning a trip to Paris and hoping to meet someone who can show you around the most romantic city in the world, it's worth subscribing to Tinder Plus. A month long subscription gives you access to Passport_, a feature that lets you change your location to anywhere abroad. This means you can match, chat, and make plans with people before you get there. Passport is also how people [matched with Olympians](https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2016/08/119845/tinder-passport-rio-olympics) during this summer's Rio Olympics.

## 9. Or, Adjust Your Location Settings

![text, web page, font, line, diagram,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sv/eo/gseziedg59197d1ab703e722409201_600x1068.jpg)

If you're not interested in long distance and don't have an upcoming trip, keep your distance settings on the lower side. If you run out of nearby matches, you can always increase your mileage, but keeping the location range smaller increases your chances of meeting someone nearby - and makes it easier to plan a date.

## 10. Show Someone You Really like Them

![text, poster, product, diagram, advertising,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/kj/xnt9v3i359197d1ad8062633241061_600x1068.jpg)

If someone really catches your eye on Tinder, let them know. Press the blue heart on the bottom right to send that person a Super Like. [According to Tinder](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/updated-introducing-super-like-a-new-type-of-swipe/), you're three times more likely to match with that person and have a longer conversation with them than someone you just right swipe on.

## 11. Get a Boost

![color, text, font, product, screenshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/la/kp3f7mu159197d1b4e9df892706679_600x1067.jpg)

Tinder's recently expanded [Boost feature](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/skip-the-line-with-tinder-boost/) puts you in front of the swipe line. For $3, you can make sure your profile is seen by 10 times the number of people as it usually is. A boost only lasts for 30 minutes, so choose your timing wisely.

## 12. Extend Your Time

![text, font, product, advertising, diagram,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/hh/uhsr9z4e59197d1b74420386568907_600x1067.jpg)

On Bumble, where matches expire after 24 hours, you can use the [Boost](https://web.archive.org/web/http://blog.bumble.com/bumbleblog/introducing-bumble-boost) tool to get an extra day. Spending $10 for one month of Boosts will also let you rematch with expired matches.

## 13. Get Additional Matches

_ Bonfire_ is an app designed to increase your match list on Tinder. Once you've downloaded and synced it with your Tinder account, you'll see anyone who has already liked your profile and have the option to match with them. Plus, you can look back through your own "like" history.

## 14. Do a Background Check

_ We've all seen Catfish. Stud or Dud_ is a free app that combats catfishing by allowing you to make sure that a match is the real deal. You can search a match by name, email, or phone number. Add your location, and you can see if said person has a criminal record, as well as their age and marital status.

## 15. Verify Your Photo

_ Bumble's new "catfish-killing" technology_ lets you take extra precautions by verifying your photo - and seeing which matches have verified theirs, too. All you have to do is press the verify button on your profile, mimic a pose shown in a photo, and send that photo to Bumble administrators. They review the photo and give you speedy verification (or, let you know if you need to retake the image).

## 16. Say It with GIFs

![text, font, calendar, brand, presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/gu/kvrz2rj459197d1e50c11900468095_427x760.jpg)

A GIF can be worth a thousand words - and can add some humor to your messages. To find the perfect one for the sentiment you're trying to convey, just tap the [blue GIF box](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/say-more-with-tinders-new-messaging-features/) to the left of your message bar.

## 17. Link Your Spotify Account

_ Sharing music tastes with a match isn't a must, but it does give you something else to use as an icebreaker. Go to your profile settings in [Tinder](https://www.tinderpressroom.com/tinder-is-swiping-up-the-volume-with-spotify/) or Bumble_ and link your Spotify account. From there, you can choose which artists you want to show up. So no, you don't need to let matches see all your guilty pleasures.

## 18. Double Date

_ Tinder Social_ is a good way to go out with friends but also meet new people and potential new matches. First, go to your settings and turn on "Show me on Tinder Social." Then, on your main swiping screen, toggle over from the flame logo above your profile photo to the friends symbol. Doing so puts you in Social mode, where you can create a group with friends and chat with other groups about meeting up.

## 19. Open with a Conversation Starter

![text, font, product, diagram, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/aw/ep0eidxq59197d207d3c2370494290_600x1068.jpg)

It doesn't get more basic than this. When it comes down to it, no matter how many boosts or premium settings you try, a successful match is all about the conversation. Your chances of getting a response to "Hey, what's up?" are slim. Stand out and show off your smarts with an engaging question about something in your match's profile or a reference to something that's happened in the news that week.
