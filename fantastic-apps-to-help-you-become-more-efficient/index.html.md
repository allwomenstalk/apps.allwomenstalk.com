# Need to Be More Efficient? Theres an App for That ...

There are so many apps to help you become more efficient. Whether you need help with time management, having an easier way to create grocery lists, or even working out, there's an app for everything. Here are some cool apps to help you become more efficient:

## 1. Out of Milk

![Out of Milk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/sg/bgvrpz9l_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/grocery-shopping-list-out/id564974992?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Have you ever gone grocery shopping and realized you forgot the one item you headed out for in the first place? Yeah, me too. This app is here to change things! Out of Milk is the best app for creating and sharing grocery lists. You can even share and email your lists with friends and family instantly, making it easy to divide shopping duties. This is just one of many cool apps to help you become more efficient.

## 2. Word Lens

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
Word Lens makes translating things SO much easier and allows you to see the world in your language. You can translate printed words using your phone camera, in real time! How incredible is that?! The app offers in-app purchases for different language packs you might need/want, like "English-Russian," "English-Portuguese," and so many more. The cool thing about the app is that no network is required - you'll get results immediately, no matter where you are in the world.

## 3. Strides

![Strides](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/fw/yo9h3bqf_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/VtpOKz)
**Price:** $3.99
Strides provides a convenient place to track all of your goals and habits. Setting your goals is easy - all you have to do is choose what it is you want to track, name your goal, and then edit values, dates, and reminders. It'll definitely help keep you motivated! You can track your goals, habits, milestones, etc. It might be a bit pricy but it's a really good investment!

## 4. First Aid by American Red Cross

![First Aid by American Red Cross](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/tg/x0lwsj9g_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/first-aid-by-american-red/id529160691?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
It's always better to be safe than sorry, right? Having this app will help you deal with whatever emergency comes your way! It features videos, interactive quizzes, and step-by-step advice about first aid. The app is also integrated with 9-1-1 so you can call EMA right from the app! You'll find safety tips for everything, which is great because you can never be too cautious or prepared.

## 5. 7 Minute Workout

![7 Minute Workout](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/ao/alv53tt2_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/7-minute-workout-challenge/id680170305?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $1.99
Exercise is a very important and should be part of your daily routine! Granted, not everyone has a lot of free time throughout their day. That's where this app comes in handy. All you have to do is spare seven minutes and don't even try to say that you don't have the time! You can always set your alarm seven minutes earlier to make sure you have the time to squeeze this in. Getting a workout in will definitely make you feel great for the rest of the day!

## 6. Account Tracker

![Account Tracker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/ks/a6vdl5a5_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/account-tracker/id325834957?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** $2.99
With this app, you can keep track of your spending and monitor all your balances across multiple accounts. It even has some cool features like alerts, bill reminders, multi-currency support, and more. It's the easy way to take control of your finances!

## 7. 30/30

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
This is one of the coolest time-management apps! You can set up a list of tasks and add a length of time for each item. When you start the timer, it will tell you when to move on to the next task. It will motivate you to do as much as you can in the time that you've given yourself!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many fantastic apps to help you become more efficient. Honestly, it still amazes me that there is truly an app for everything! LOL, can you think of any other apps that promote efficiency?
