# 7 Awesome Apps That'll Help You Discover New Things ...

There are so many fantastic apps that will help you discover new things. You won't be surprised to know that there really is an app for everything! Whether you want to discover new music, people, events, these apps will help you. Check out some cool apps that will help you discover new things. Let me know your favorites!

## 1. Yplan

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
This app is so awesome. YPlan helps you discover and book the best events around town! For now, YPlan's app caters only to New York City, San Francisco, and London. However, I have a good feeling they'll be expanding their areas very soon! If you find yourself asking your friends, "what are we doing tonight?", then YPlan probably has your answer. It's one of the awesome apps that will help you discover new things.

## 2. Around Me

![Around Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/ek/da36don1_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/aroundme/id290051590?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Around Me is definitely a convenient app to have. It allows you to quickly find out information about your surroundings. It identifies your position and allows you to choose the nearest bank, bar, gas station, etc. When you choose whatever category you're looking for, Around Me will show you all the businesses near you along with the distance from your location.

## 3. Taphunter

![Taphunter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/mj/odu80l57_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/taphunter-craft-beer-finder/id512023104?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
This app is for anyone who's a beer lover. TapHunter shows you what's on tap at the best local brewpubs, beer bars, and all other locations. You can search by beer, location, or brewery! If you find yourself bored this weekend, here's a fun thing to go out and do with your friends! Beer tasting, anyone?

## 4. Shazam

![Shazam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/mv/igs655ss_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/shazam/id284993459?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
One thing this app helps you discover is great music. I know it's a popular app but still, it's worthy of a spot on this list! The app recognizes music and media that's playing around you. You can tap the Shazam button to "tag" a song. Once you tag it, you can do an in-app purchase, share it, or comment. You can even watch the song's YouTube videos and check out the artist's biography.

## 5. Highlight

![Highlight](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/vi/ulul7ylp_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/highlight-people-nearby/id441534409?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
It seems like the easiest way to meet people is through social media. With Highlight, you can discover new people around you! The app is a fun and simple way to learn more about the people who surround you. If someone's standing near you and they also have the Highlight app, their profile will show up on your phone. You'll be able to see their name, photos, mutual friends, and more. When you meet someone new, the app helps you see what things you have in common with them.

## 6. Krrb

![Krrb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zc/pb/cx3o5og1_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/krrb-local-classifieds/id445334765?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Krrb (pronounced "curb") is a hyperlocal classifieds made just for you. On the app, you can thrift, scavenge, rummage, and discover local treasures. You can see what's around you while walking down the street! On the app, you can contact sellers or create your own listings. It's the perfect app for people who love unique finds!

## 7. Foodspotting

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
This app is your visual guide to awesome food and where to find it. Instead of just seeing restaurant reviews, you can see recommended dishes and see what other foodspotters have to say about it. The app is powered by people who take pictures of their food, so deciding where you want to go will be easy!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome apps that'll help you discover new things. Which apps are your favorite to use - what do they help you discover? Of the list, which app is your new favorite?
