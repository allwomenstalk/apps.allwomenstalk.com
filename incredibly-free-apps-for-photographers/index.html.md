## 1. Dropbox

![Dropbox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iu/qn/z9ohjxyb_600x532.jpg)

Dropbox is truly the front runner of file storage and organization; it should be a staple for anyone in the field. This app allows users to retrieve pictures, documents, and videos from any source, regardless of location. This is perfect for clients who do not live locally or even for publication purposes. Dropbox is easily accessible with user-friendly capabilities. Users can now share links of extremely large files without the dreaded task of trying to upload additional attachments. Nix the confusion and panic of constantly remembering to download photos from your home computer and join in on the freedom of dropbox mobility.

## 2. Flint Mobile

![Flint Mobile](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/f2/jkh5rlgr_600x1065.jpg)

There is a numerous array of available point-of-sale (POS) apps, but if you’re someone who would rather not hassle with credit card readers or waiting several weeks for your additional gadget accessories to arrive, Flint is for you! This app brings simplicity back to mobile transactions by providing the choice of either typing or scanning the credit card number, security code, and billing zip code. Moreover, the customer can rest assured because there is no additional need for photos to be taken or saved to the phone for any reason unlike many other POS apps. Flint supports multiple transactions, accepts cash, checks, invoices, and even allows for distribution of coupons, all in one place.

## 3. Photographers Contract Maker LITE

![Photographers Contract Maker LITE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/e9/hqew2zmx_562x900.jpg)

If you're not using Contract Maker, you must incorporate this into your business plan immediately. This app takes away the hardship of having to carry an excessive amount of model release papers to each job location. Take away the nerve-racking thought of losing this necessity and go paperless! Create, edit, sign, and email photography contracts and releases right from your phone, on site. In fact, if you like this free version, you can upgrade for a minimal fee to receive an unlimited amount of custom contracts and releases.

## 4. Magic Hour

![Magic Hour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/on/n7/jdevup2n_600x1065.jpg)

As any professional photographer knows, the ‘magic hour’ to obtain phenomenal images is during sunrise and sunset. The problem, however, is that few can remember when this time actually is, especially if you’re shooting on location. Fortunately this app takes away that painstaking frustration and informs you of the Magic Hours of any location! Now you can remain worry-free when traveling to new venues and focus on the real priority- capturing priceless moments.

## 5. Evernote

![Evernote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y2/gw/cv4mdbe7_600x872.jpg)

Whether trying to become a professional or remain as a freelance photographer, Evernote is an essential app to remain organized. Since this vocation requires mobility, Evernote supports flexibility by providing a modern-day workspace that's also portable. This app is truly the epitome for self-productivity by allowing users to takes notes, create notebooks or albums, manage expenses, plan traveling, tackle checklists, share ideas and resources, increase connections by turning business cards into contacts, and capture ideas while still relevant. This app can be accessed offline, from anywhere in the world.

## 6. Red Stamp Cards

![Red Stamp Cards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/lu/qx5shinz_600x749.jpg)

Among all the recent online methods to administer self-marketing products, Red Stamp is that ideal option for photographers. This novel app is an insidious advertising opportunity to increase self-revenue while simultaneously enhancing your publicity. Birthdays, holidays, invitations, post-cards, notes, and announcements are endless, and few people have time to send out a physical card. Red Stamp allows you to create various personalized forms of cards with pictures, right from your phone. For a very low price you can charge clients for this service while having the option to send it through the mail, email, and text messaging or even post it to a social media account from any location worldwide. Draw in more cliental by creating package options in bulk for lower prices.

## 7. Mad Mimi

![Mad Mimi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v5/ab/yyogmgu9_600x1065.jpg)

Never again will you be confronted with a potential client requesting more information about your work and have to wait until you get home to send it. Mad Mimi allows you to create, send, share, and track email newsletters about one of your many upcoming photo-shoots, photography shows, or events, all from the convenience of your phone. This app even further simplifies this service by providing design themes and layouts for easier creativity. After emailing recipients a newsletter about your upcoming event, check its status throughout the entire day while out on location.

To date, the majority of apps categorized under photography are primarily for photo editing and photo altering. Though these are popular among Facebook and Instagram users, it can be quite overwhelming for someone seeking useful applications to grow their small business. There are few, if any, lists such as this, which suggest a well-rounded depiction of useful apps that are valuable for growing a unique business like photography; considering the exponential rate of online webpages becoming available for self-marketed products, it is highly suggested that anyone who is serious about growing their business, should strongly consider making these apps a fundamental part of their development plan.
