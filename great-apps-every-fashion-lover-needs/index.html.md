# 7 Great Apps Every Fashion Lover Needs ...

There are so many fantastic apps for fashion lovers. Whether you love to just browse photos or shop the latest trends, these apps are definitely worth checking out. You'll find inspiration everywhere! Check out some awesome apps for fashion lovers.

## 1. Rent the Runway

![Rent the Runway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/rj/nizqknoc_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rent-the-runway/id672853806?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

The Rent The Runway app is one of the best apps for fashion lovers. You'll find designer dresses, accessories, and handbag rentals - all at 90% OFF retail prices. How amazing is that?! When you rent a dress, it even includes a free second size to ensure you get the perfect fit! New styles and designers are posted every week. This is a must-have whenever you have a special occasion coming up!

## 2. Chicfeed

![Chicfeed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nx/u8/hlqw9hi8_332x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chicfeed/id338930115?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

Chicfeed is awesome. The app brings together photos from the web's top style/fashion blogs and puts them in one convenient place. There are six blogs that are currently included in the blog! All produce cool street style pictures that are sure to inspire your next outfit.

## 3. Fashion Kaleidoscope

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

This app is really cool because not only does it show you great outfits from around the world, but it shows you where you can buy them! On the app, you'll find inspiring images from street style, fashion blogging, celebrities and more! You can browse outfits and save your favorites - then share them on Pinterest, Twitter and Facebook! You can find items that are on sale and the stores that offer the cheapest options.

## 4. Lookbook

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

If you're looking for some fashion inspiration, Lookbook is a great place to find it! You'll find inspiration from real people all around the world. It's a great place to find ideas for your next outfit!

## 5. Poshmark

![Poshmark](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hg/pg/i8hpnux4_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/poshmark-buy-sell-fashion/id470412147?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

This app is one of my favorites! It's becoming one of the leading destinations to buy and sell fashion. On the app, you can list an item for sale in just seconds. Or, you can shop! They've got everything you're looking for - trust me. New items are added every day from big designers. You can also visit the app's "parties," where you can join thousands of women in a themed virtual shopping spree.

## 6. Snapette

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_

Snapette is such a fun shopping app! It's great for finding designer fashion, shoes, handbags and even accessories! You can set up the app to send you real-time alerts about new stores, any special discounts and exclusive giveaways from over 700 designer brands! It's an awesome and convenient way to shop from the comfort of your own home or while you're on the go!

## 7. Style.com

![Style.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/eu/qwe5ckmi_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/style-com/id289380413?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)

The Style.com app is the best way to keep up with the latest runway fashion. On the app, you get the latest ready-to-wear, couture, resort and menswear shows from New York, Paris, Milan, London; the world's fashion capitals! In addition to every catwalk look, the app offers up-to-the-moment party coverage and industry news! You can even watch videos of runway shows and designer and model profiles!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome apps that are perfect for fashion lovers. Which apps do you use to stay up to date on all things fashion? Of the list, which app is your new favorite?
