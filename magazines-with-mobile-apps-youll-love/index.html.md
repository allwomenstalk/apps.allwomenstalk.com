# 7 Magazines with Mobile Apps Youll Love ...

There are so many awesome magazines with mobile apps. I don't always have time to go out and buy the printed edition so it makes me happy to have these apps. They're all free to download but you must eventually subscribe to the magazine to continue getting access to their issues. Check out some of my favorite magazines with mobile apps.

## 1. Cosmopolitan

![Cosmopolitan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/yw/hb56msop_320x568.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/cosmopolitan-magazine-us/id466052219?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
Who doesn't love a good Cosmo mag? When you download their free app, you'll get your first issue for free! After that, you can choose to subscribe to their issues. You can pay $1.99 monthly (automatically renewed until canceled) or you can pay $20.00 yearly (also automatically renewed). On the app you can download all their hot tips on beauty, relationships, sex, fashion, and even fun interactive elements that you won't find in their printed magazine. If you're an avid Cosmo reader, you won't regret getting this app. It's one of the best magazines with mobile apps!

## 2. Seventeen

![Seventeen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/sq/m6mh9crz_360x480.jpg)

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/HgKwwt)
**Price:** Free
By starting a trial subscription, you'll get your first issue of Seventeen Magazine for free! In the app, you'll find tons of awesome style ideas, beauty tricks, juicy guy confessions, and real-life stories. 1-month subscriptions (automatically renewed) are available for $1.99 while yearly subscriptions (also automatically renewed unless canceled) are available for $14.99. I'm subscribed to Seventeen and I absolutely love it! I think it's so worth it because this magazine rules.

## 3. Popstar! Magazine

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/nluIoB)
**Price:** Free
This is for all tween magazine readers! When I was younger, Popstar! was definitely my go-to magazine to get all the latest celebrity gossip. With their app, you can view all posts that have been posted to their website. This includes all breaking news! You can watch videos, read posts, etc. all from their app! It's free so it's definitely worth checking out if you want to know the latest on One Direction, R5, etc.

## 4. INSTYLE

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
Want to keep up with the latest trends while you're on the go? Download InStyle Magazine's mobile app! On the app, you'll get tips on what's hot and how to wear it. You can choose to subscribe for 1-month for $1.99 (automatically renewed until canceled), 1-year for $23.99 (automatically renewed) or you can choose to purchase a single issue for $4.99. There will be no automatic renewal for that! If you want to stay in the loop in the fashion world, this Magazine is the one for you.

## 5. US Weekly

_ **On iTunes:** itunes.apple.com_
**Price:** Free
If you're more into the tabloids and celebrity gossip, US Weekly is the magazine for you. When you download the app, you can enjoy a 1 month trial! That means four issues - for free! After that, you can choose to sign up for a one-month, six-month, or one-year subscription. Single issues are also available for purchase! In the app, you'll get celebrity news and incredible photography of all your favorite celebrities.

## 6. Elle

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/elle-magazine-us/id394838174?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
When you subscribe to ELLE Magazine, you'll get your first issue for free! With the app, you'll experience the very latest in fashion, trends, culture, and beauty in a beautiful and interactive edition! On the app, you'll also get exclusive videos including commentaries from ELLE editors who take you behind-the-shoot with your favorite artists, celebs, and designers. You can choose between a 1-month ($1.99) and a 1-year ($19.99) subscription which are both automatically renewed until canceled.

## 7. Rolling Stone

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rolling-stone-magazine/id584432649?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
**Price:** Free
If you're a music/pop culture lover, you should definitely look into subscribing to Rolling Stone magazine. I had it for a while and loved it! On the app, you'll get all the breaking music news, hot album and movie reviews, must-read rock star profiles and more. With the download, you'll get a 1 month free trial subscription! After that, you can sign up for a monthly or yearly subscription.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many magazines with mobile apps that you'll love. If you're an avid reader of any of these magazines but can't always buy the printed edition, you can stream it all on the go! Of the list, which one is your favorite magazine?
