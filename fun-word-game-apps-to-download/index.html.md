# 7 Fun Word Game Apps to Download ...

There are so many fun (yet extremely frustrating) word game apps you should download. I love playing these because they're a lot of fun but at the same time, they challenge your mind. That's the best, right? Check out some of these fun word game apps. Let me know which ones your favorites are.

## 1. Ruzzle

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/sa/eu805am8_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/g8QHm2)
Price: Free
It's scary how addictive Ruzzle can be. You can play against your friends or match with random opponents! The purpose of the app is to find as many words as possible in two minutes. It's definitely challenging! Ruzzle is just one of the fun word game apps worth downloading.

## 2. Word to Word

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cp/fh/vcfjjdu7_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/word-to-word-fun-addictive/id441894920?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Price: Free
Word to Word is fun and very, very addictive. All you need to do is match words that could be associated with each other. They could be opposites, mean the same thing, be compound words, etc. They just need to be related. It definitely makes you think.

## 3. 7 Little Words

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/09/n5f1k4cs_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/7-little-words/id431434152?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Price: Free
When I first downloaded 7 Little Words, it took over my life. It's extremely frustrating (I'm not exaggerating here, people!) but it definitely makes you want to keep going until you solve it all. If you enjoy crosswords, word finds, and anagram games, this is the game for you. Each puzzle consists of 7 clues, 7 mystery words, and 20 letter groups. Decipher the clues and solve the words.

## 4. Words with Friends

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/zf/bdeyx71r_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/words-with-friends-free/id321916506?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Price: Free
Remember when this was ALL you and your friends could play? It pretty much took over everyone's lives last year. It's still a really fun app though. You can connect with your friends or discover new ones by matching up with random opponents. You can unlock your win/loss record and see how you compare against them. It's definitely an app that stimulates your mind to unleash your awesome vocabulary.

## 5. Crosswords

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/pc/cyyh9vsu_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/crosswords/id590261677?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Price: Free
On this app, you can do hundreds of crosswords anytime and anywhere. The app is super fun and so easy to use. Every week, the app will give you a free puzzle. You can hide your keyboard so that the puzzle takes up the entire screen. You can write in 'pen' or 'pencil', receive hints, and choose to see your puzzles in one of the three languages, English, German, and Spanish.

## 6. Wordfeud

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/pj/hxiqxdj1_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wordfeud/id428312806?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
Price: Free
Wordfeud is a multiplayer puzzle game where you can challenge friends or random opponents. It's really cool because you can play in up to 30 separate games simultaneously. You can create and place words on the board and earn points for creativity and placing letters on the high scoring placement tiles. You can search for your friends or let the app match you up with a random opponent - you can even chat with them.

## 7. Boggle

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/ky/me36j6ri_568x320.jpg)

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com
Price: Free
This app is free but it offers in-app purchases. It's such an awesome word search game. You can 'boggle' in three different modes and compete with up to four players. There's a timer while you play, which can be either totally infuriating or a huge motivator. I guess it's up to you to decide. You can unlock new scenarios with each accomplishment.
