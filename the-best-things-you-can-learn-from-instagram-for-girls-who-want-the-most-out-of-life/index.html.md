# The Best Things You Can Learn  from Instagram for Girls Who Want the Most out of Life ...

Instagram is a pretty common app for the younger crowd. It's a great way to keep up with friends, follow celebs and watch hilarious videos. That's not all it can do though because there are tons of **things you can learn from using Instagram** that will make life so much better. So next time you log in, try one of these cool things. Life has never been so good when you use Instagram to learn something new. Have fun!

## 1. Learn to Cook

![food, dessert, snack, breakfast, fruit,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s7/hy/la14sw0159821f48c56c0279577208_600x729.jpg)

There is so much food porn on Instagram these days, but did you know that you can learn to cook there too? Follow foodie accounts and you'll find recipes and how-to videos that will turn you into a master chef in no time at all. How cool is that?

## 2. Learn to Stay on Top of the Trends

![eyewear, fashion model, shoulder, vision care, sunglasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/lv/kj1lgdgb59821f6a9a5f7460311783_600x750.jpg)

If you're not seeing food on Instagram, chances are that you're seeing clothes instead. If you want to rock a new trend or style an outfit without going shopping, turn to the experts here and you're all set. There's fashion inspiration for everyone and it's so easy to find.

## 3. Learn How to Wear Makeup

![eyebrow, beauty, eyelash, forehead, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/qi/x7sporvx59821fce95cc1594364311_600x750.jpg)

Whether you're new to makeup or you just want a new look, you can totally learn how by using Instagram. Watch videos and get inspired by lovely trends that you can try on your own face. You're going to be the best looking girl in town!

## 4. Learn to Take Outstanding Photos

![shoulder, fashion model, outerwear, socialite, blazer,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/a5/e913wome59821f8e44285253962949_600x750.jpg)

You might not be able to find too much expert advice on taking photos on Instagram, but you can sure find all the inspiration you need. Look through the photos on your feed and you can learn about lighting, staging and cool backgrounds just by paging through.

## 5. Learn to Start a Business

![beauty, human hair color, eyebrow, blond, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/b6/gup193ep59821fe175920979096898_600x690.jpg)

It's not going to happen overnight, but you could turn your photography skills into money if you reach the right audience. Whether you snap photos of food or landscapes, being seen by a pro could mean dollars in your pocket. So build your following and reach out to others who share your interests. You never know what might happen!

## 6. Learn to Be More Accepting

![swimwear, sunglasses, vacation, fun, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/kb/k6rhra1359821fabd7ff1578805151_600x750.jpg)

Even if you live in a small town and don't see the world often, you can learn from other cultures simply by making new friends online. Obviously, you want to be careful who you connect with, but you can see more of the world and the people in it by reaching out via Instagram to populations near and far.

## 7. Learn How to Travel

![car, vehicle, shoulder, motor vehicle, automotive design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bw/yf/sgc5ngpg598220054bbe1270945984_600x749.jpg)

If Instagram does anything, it certainly makes you want to see more of the world. By connecting with people and places, you can figure out where to go, what to see and who to see it with. If you need a vacay, Instagram is sure to teach you the best place to go.

Do you use Instagram? What has it taught you?
