# 7 Practical Apps for Couples That Are Practically Genius!

There are quite a few amazing apps for couples that will help you strengthen that special bond that you share. When you love someone, you want to be as connected to them as possible so you won’t miss anything, no matter how small or insignificant it may be. These apps are especially practical when you and your better half are miles apart due to all kinds of valid reasons. They will help you stay connected to your loved one and they will also teach you how to deal with various aspects of your relationship, from getting to know each other better to solving conflicts and settling fights, so you can be a better partner. Here are 7 practical apps for couples that are practically genius:

## 1. Couple

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
This app will provide you a more intimate way to share your life and to stay in touch with your favorite person in the world. It’s free, it’s fun and it’s completely private. It allows you and your partner to share messages, videos and even draw together. This is also by far one of the best apps for long distance couples, but it also works perfectly if you and your significant other live in the same town.

## 2. Fix a Fight

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
Even though this app is currently in repair since its creators considered it to be a bit unstable, it’s still one of the best apps for couples that you should install on your phone or tablet. It’s quite simple to use and it guides you through the steps needed to repair your relationship after a big fight. You could think of it as free couples therapy. Just give it a try and see for yourself how amazing this app is.

## 3. Icebreak for Couples

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
This is one of the best apps for couples to stay close and stay in love. It’s a great way to say things to your partner that you may not get a chance to say otherwise. It’s also perfect for young couples since it allows them to share their answers to personality questions and hypotheticals.

## 4. Avocado

_ On iTunes at: itunes.apple.com_
This is a private, fun and secure app for couples to stay connected. It’s great for married couples since it allows you to send private messages to your significant other, share lists and even a calendar, doodle and put stickers on photos and even share a media gallery. This couples app will help you and your significant other stay connected all the time, whether you are in the same room or continents away.

## 5. Simply Us

![Simply Us](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/f9/vbov31xy5594510a83677800813162_320x544.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/o9Gnp1)
This app helps couples organize their life together by allowing them to share calendars, memories, lists and messages and it’s great for managing busy schedules, agendas, chores, tasks, reminders, family schedules, checklists and more. Basically, it stores all that’s important to your relationship in one place.

## 6. WhereAreYou App

![WhereAreYou App](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/ut/t6qs0hh15594510ad789b293179061_320x568.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/app/where-are-you-app/id608238999?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&amp%3Bign-mpt=uo%3D4)
This app will allow you to find out where your sweetheart is with only a text message. For this to work, the other party doesn’t have to have this app installed on their phone but each location request has to be sent and approved separately, so it requires the consent to be located. It's one of the safest location sharing apps in the world.

## 7. Between

![Between](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lo/n3/qs68vpwd5594510aca8c9054501301_322x540.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/app/between/id458035189?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&vcnc_referer=appbetween)
This app is a private, fun and secure way for couples to store and share their memories. It allows you to chat with your significant other and it has specially designed stickers and stamps that will help you communicate your feelings better.

There are a lot of amazing apps for couples that you can use to increase the intimacy in your relationship and to strengthen that special bond that you share with your significant other. I just mentioned a few in this little article, but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. What apps for couples do you use? Please tell us more about them in the comments section!
